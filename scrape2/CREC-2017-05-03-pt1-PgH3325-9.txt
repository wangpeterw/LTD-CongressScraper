       Congress has the power to enact this legislation pursuant 
     to the following:
       Article I, Section 8, Clause 3 of the U.S. Constitution The 
     Congress shall have power to lay and collect taxes, duties, 
     imposts and exercises, to pay the debts and provide for the 
     common defense and general welfare of the United States; but 
     all duties, imposts and exercises shall be uniform throughout 
     the United States
