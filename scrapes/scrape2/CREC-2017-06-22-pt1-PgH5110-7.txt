            By Mr. GOODLATTE:
        H.R. 3003.
        Congress has the power to enact this legislation pursuant 
     to the following:
        Clause 4 of Section 8 of Article I--The Congress shall 
     have the Power to establish a uniform Rule of Naturalization, 
     and uniform Laws on the subject of Bankruptcies throughout 
     the United States.
        Clause 1 of Section 8 of Article I--The Congress shall 
     have Power to lay and collect Taxes, Duties, Imposts and 
     Excises, to pay the Debts and provide for the common Defence 
     and general Welfare of the United States.
