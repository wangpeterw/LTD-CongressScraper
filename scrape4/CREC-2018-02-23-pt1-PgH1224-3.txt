                COMMUNICATION FROM THE DEMOCRATIC LEADER

  The SPEAKER pro tempore laid before the House the following 
communication from the Honorable Nancy Pelosi, Democratic Leader:

                                                February 23, 2018.
     Hon. Paul D. Ryan,
     Speaker of the House of Representatives,
     Washington, DC.
       Dear Speaker Ryan: Pursuant to section 30422(b)(3) of the 
     Bipartisan Budget Act of 2018 (Pub. L. 115-123), I am pleased 
     to appoint the following Members to serve on the Joint Select 
     Committee on Solvency of Multiemployer Pension Plans:
       The Honorable Richard Neal of Massachusetts
       The Honorable Bobby Scott of Virginia
       The Honorable Donald Norcross of New Jersey
       The Honorable Debbie Dingell of Michigan
       Thank you for your attention to these appointments.
           Sincerely,
                                                     Nancy Pelosi,
     Democratic Leader.
