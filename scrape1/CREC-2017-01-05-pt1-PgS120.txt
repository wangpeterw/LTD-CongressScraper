                    AUTHORITY FOR COMMITTEES TO MEET

  Mr. BLUNT. Mr. President, I have five requests for committees to meet 
during today's session of the Senate. They have the approval of the 
Majority and Minority leaders.

[[Page S121]]

  Pursuant to Rule XXVI, paragraph 5(a), of the Standing Rules of the 
Senate, the following committees are authorized to meet during today's 
session of the Senate:


                      committee on armed services

  Mr. President, I ask unanimous consent that the Committee on Armed 
Services be authorized to meet during the session of the Senate on 
January 5, 2017, at 9:30 a.m.


                     committee on foreign relations

  Mr. President, I ask unanimous consent that the Committee on Foreign 
Relations be authorized to meet during the session of the Senate on 
January 5, 2017 at 3 p.m., to conduct a classified briefing entitled 
``Recent Administration Actions in Response to Russian Hacking and 
Harassment of U.S. Diplomats.''


                    select committee on intelligence

  Mr. President, I ask unanimous consent that the Select Committee on 
Intelligence be authorized to meet during the session of the Senate on 
January 5, 2017, at 2 p.m. in room SH-219 of the Hart Senate Office 
Building.
