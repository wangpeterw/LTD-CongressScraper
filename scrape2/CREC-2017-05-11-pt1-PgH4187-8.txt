                         SENATE BILLS REFERRED

  Bills of the Senate of the following titles were taken from the 
Speaker's table and, under the rule, referred as follows:

       S. 140. An act to amend the White Mountain Apache Tribe 
     Water Right Quantification Act of 2010 to clarify the use of 
     amounts in the WMAT Settlement Fund; to the Committee on 
     Natural Resources.
       S. 249. An act to provide that the pueblo of Santa Clara 
     may lease for 99 years certain restricted land, and for other 
     purposes; to the Committee on Natural Resources.
