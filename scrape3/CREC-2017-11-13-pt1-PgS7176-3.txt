                            VOTE EXPLANATION

 Mr. MENENDEZ. Mr. President, I was unavailable for rollcall 
vote No. 270, on the nomination of Derek Kan, of California, to be 
Under Secretary of Transportation for Policy. Had I been present, I 
would have voted nay.
  Mr. President, I was unavailable for rollcall vote No. 271, on the 
motion to invoke cloture on Steven Gill Bradbury, of Virginia, to be 
general counsel of the Department of Transportation. Had I been 
present, I would have voted nay.
