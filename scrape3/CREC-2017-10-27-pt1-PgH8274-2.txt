                               MEMORIALS

  Under clause 3 of rule XII,

       141. The SPEAKER presented a memorial of the Senate of the 
     State of Ohio, relative to Senate Resolution No. 59, to 
     encourage the President and the Congress of the United States 
     and the United States Office of Management and Budget to 
     support plans to upgrade the Soo Locks at Sault Ste. Marie, 
     Michigan, and encourage the United States Army Corps of 
     Engineers to take expeditious action in preparing an Economic 
     Reevaluation Report; which was referred to the Committee on 
     Transportation and Infrastructure.
