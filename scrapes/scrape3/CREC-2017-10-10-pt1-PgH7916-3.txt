                    BILL PRESENTED TO THE PRESIDENT

  Karen L. Haas, Clerk of the House, reported that on October 6, 2017, 
she presented to the President of the United States, for his approval, 
the following bill:

       H.R. 1117. To require the Administrator of the Federal 
     Emergency Management Agency to submit a report regarding 
     certain plans regarding assistance to applicants and grantees 
     during the response to an emergency or disaster.
