                    AMENDMENTS SUBMITTED AND PROPOSED

       SA 217. Mr. SULLIVAN (for Mr. Roberts (for himself and Mrs. 
     McCaskill)) proposed an amendment to the bill H.R. 1238, to 
     amend the Homeland Security Act of 2002 to make the Assistant 
     Secretary of Homeland Security for Health Affairs responsible 
     for coordinating the efforts of the Department of Homeland 
     Security related to food, agriculture, and veterinary defense 
     against terrorism, and for other purposes.
