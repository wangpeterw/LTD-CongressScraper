                   AMENDMENTS SUBMITTED AND PROPOSED

       SA 1577. Mr. McCONNELL (for Mr. Cassidy (for himself, Mr. 
     Bennet, Mr. Blunt, and Mr. Franken)) proposed an amendment to 
     the bill H.R. 304, to amend the Controlled Substances Act 
     with regard to the provision of emergency medical services.
       SA 1578. Mr. McCONNELL (for Mrs. Ernst) proposed an 
     amendment to the resolution S. Res. 234, recognizing the 
     Sailors and Marines who sacrificed their lives for ship and 
     shipmates while fighting the devastating 1967 fire onboard 
     USS Forrestal and, during the week of the 50th anniversary of 
     the tragic event, commemorating the efforts of those who 
     survived.
