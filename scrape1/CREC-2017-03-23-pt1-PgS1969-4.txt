                        ENROLLED BILLS PRESENTED

  The Secretary of the Senate reported that on today, March 23, 2017, 
she had presented to the President of the United States the following 
enrolled bill:

       S. 305. An act to amend title 4, United States Code, to 
     encourage the display of the flag of the United States on 
     National Vietnam War Veterans Day.
