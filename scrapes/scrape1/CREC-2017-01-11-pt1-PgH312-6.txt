                          PLEDGE OF ALLEGIANCE

  The SPEAKER. Will the gentleman from Rhode Island (Mr. Langevin) come 
forward and lead the House in the Pledge ofAllegiance.
  Mr. LANGEVIN led the Pledge of Allegiance as follows:

       I pledge allegiance to the Flag of theUnited States of 
     America, and to the Republic for which it stands, one nation 
     under God, indivisible, with liberty and justicefor all.
