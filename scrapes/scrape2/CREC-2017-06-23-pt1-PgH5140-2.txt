            By Mrs. McMORRIS RODGERS:
        H.R. 3043.
        Congress has the power to enact this legislation pursuant 
     to the following:
        The Constitutional authority in which this bill rests is 
     the power of Congress to regulate Commerce as enumerated by 
     Article I, Section 8, Clause 3 as applied to waterways for 
     the development of hydroelectric power and flood control.
