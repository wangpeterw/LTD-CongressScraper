                         MESSAGE FROM THE HOUSE

  At 4 p.m., a message from the House of Representatives, delivered by 
Mr. Novotny, one of its reading clerks, announced that pursuant to 
section 4(b) of the World War I Centennial Commission Act (Public Law 
112-272), the Minority Leader appoints Ms. Maria Zoe Dunning, of San 
Francisco, California, to the World War I Centennial Commission; Ms. 
Maria Zoe Dunning to replace Mr. Robert Dalessandro appointed in 2013 
who resigned from the Commission.
