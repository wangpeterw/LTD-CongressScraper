         A BILL AND JOINT RESOLUTION PRESENTED TO THE PRESIDENT

  Karen L. Haas, Clerk of the House, reported that on October 25, 2017, 
she presented to the President of the United States, for his approval, 
the following bill and joint resolution:

       H.R. 2266. Making additional supplemental appropriations 
     for disaster relief requirements for the fiscal year ending 
     September 30, 2018, and for other purposes.
       H.J. Res. 111. Providing for congressional disapproval 
     under chapter 8 of title 5, United States Code, of the rule 
     submitted by Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection relating 
     to ``Arbitration Agreements''.
