                            PETITIONS, ETC.

  Under clause 3 of rule XII, petitions and papers were laid on the 
clerk's desk and referred as follows:

       80. The SPEAKER presented a petition of the Senate of the 
     Commonwealth of Puerto Rico, relative to Senate Concurrent 
     Resolution 28, to express the firm and unequivocal 
     repudiation and opposition of the Legislative Assembly of 
     Puerto Rico to HR 4202 of the United States House of 
     Representatives that proposes the application of the ``Animal 
     Welfare Act'' to United States territories and, consequently, 
     prohibits cockfights in Puerto Rico; to the Committee on 
     Agriculture.
       81. Also, a petition of the Senate of the Commonwealth of 
     Puerto Rico, relative to Senate Concurrent Resolution 1, to 
     request the United States Congress and the United States 
     Department of the Interior to take the necessary 
     administrative and legislative actions in order to provide 
     for the updating of various topographic and hydrographic maps 
     of our Island; and for other related purposes; to the 
     Committee on Natural Resources.
