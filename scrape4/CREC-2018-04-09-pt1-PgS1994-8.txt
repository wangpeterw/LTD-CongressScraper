                    CONGRATULATING SENATOR DUCKWORTH

  Mr. SCHUMER. Mr. President, it was Tammy Duckworth who first informed 
me of Senator Akaka's passing, and it shows God's rhythms of life, the 
passing of Danny Akaka, after a long service and wonderful life, and 
now we have

[[Page S1995]]

just learned of the birth of Tammy Duckworth's child. It is a 
milestone. Tammy Duckworth, a few hours ago, became the first Senator 
to give birth while in office. We are very glad to hear that she and 
her newborn daughter are happy and healthy.
