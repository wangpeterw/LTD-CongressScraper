 RESIGNATIONS AS MEMBER OF COMMITTEE ON THE JUDICIARY AND COMMITTEE ON 
                      EDUCATION AND THE WORKFORCE

  The SPEAKER pro tempore (Mr. Palmer) laid before the House the 
following resignations as a member of the Committee on the Judiciary 
and the Committee on Education and the Workforce:

                                    Congress of the United States,


                                     House of Representatives,

                                Washington, DC, February 16, 2017.
     Speaker Paul Ryan,
     Speaker of the House,
     Washington, DC.
       Dear Speaker Ryan: Due to my election to the Committee on 
     Ways and Means, this letter is to inform you that I resign my 
     seats on the House Judiciary committee and the Committee on 
     Education and the Workforce.
           Sincerely,
                                                Michael D. Bishop.

  The SPEAKER pro tempore. Without objection, the resignations are 
accepted.
  There was no objection.
