  HOUR OF MEETING ON TOMORROW AND ADJOURNMENT FROM FRIDAY, JANUARY 6, 
                    2017, TO MONDAY, JANUARY 9, 2017

  Mr. GOODLATTE. Mr. Speaker, I ask unanimous consent that when the 
House adjourns today, it adjourn to meet at noon tomorrow, and further 
when the House adjourns on that day, it adjourn to meet on Monday, 
January 9, 2017, when it shall convene at noon for morning-hour debate 
and 2 p.m. for legislative business.
  The SPEAKER pro tempore. Is there objection to the request of the 
gentleman from Virginia?
  There was no objection.
