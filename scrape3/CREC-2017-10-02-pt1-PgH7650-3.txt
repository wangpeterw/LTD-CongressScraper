   REPORT ON RESOLUTION PROVIDING FOR CONSIDERATION OF H.R. 36, PAIN-
                  CAPABLE UNBORN CHILD PROTECTION ACT

  Ms. CHENEY, from the Committee on Rules, submitted a privileged 
report (Rept. No. 115-338) on the resolution (H. Res. 548) providing 
for consideration of the bill (H.R. 36) to amend title 18, United 
States Code, to protect pain-capable unborn children, and for other 
purposes, which was referred to the House Calendar and ordered to be 
printed.
