               IRANIAN LEADERSHIP ASSET TRANSPARENCY ACT

  The SPEAKER pro tempore. The unfinished business is the vote on 
passage of the bill (H.R. 1638) to require the Secretary of the 
Treasury to submit a

[[Page H9880]]

report to the appropriate congressional committees on the estimated 
total assets under direct or indirect control by certain senior Iranian 
leaders and other figures, and for other purposes, on which the yeas 
and nays were ordered.
  The Clerk read the title of the bill.
  The SPEAKER pro tempore. The question is on the passage of the bill.
  This is a 5-minute vote.
  The vote was taken by electronic device, and there were--yeas 289, 
nays 135, not voting 7, as follows:
