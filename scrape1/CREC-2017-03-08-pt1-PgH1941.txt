             DEPARTMENT OF DEFENSE APPROPRIATIONS ACT, 2017

  The SPEAKER pro tempore. Pursuant to clause 8 of rule XX, the 
unfinished business is the vote on the passage of the bill (H.R. 1301) 
making appropriations for the Department of Defense for the fiscal year 
ending September 30, 2017, and for other purposes, on which the yeas 
and nays were ordered.
  The Clerk read the title of the bill.
  The SPEAKER pro tempore. The question is on the passage of the bill.
  The vote was taken by electronic device, and there were--yeas 371, 
nays 48, not voting 10, as follows:
