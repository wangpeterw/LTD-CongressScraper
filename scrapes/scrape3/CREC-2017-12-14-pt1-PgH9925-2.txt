  ADJOURNMENT FROM FRIDAY, DECEMBER 15, 2017, TO MONDAY, DECEMBER 18, 
                                  2017

  Mr. McCARTHY. Mr. Speaker, I ask unanimous consent that when the 
House adjourns on Friday, December 15, 2017, it adjourn to meet on 
Monday, December 18, 2017, when it shall convene at noon for morning-
hour debate and 2 p.m. for legislative business.
  The SPEAKER pro tempore. Is there objection to the request of the 
gentleman from California?
  There was no objection.
