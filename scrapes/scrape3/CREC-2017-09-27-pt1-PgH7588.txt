                               MEMORIALS

  Under clause 3 of rule XII, memorials were presented and referred as 
follows:

       126. The SPEAKER presented a memorial of the General 
     Assembly of the State of New Jersey, relative to Assembly 
     Resolution No. 254, urging the President and Congress to 
     expand access to afterschool summer meal programs and 
     streamline the application process for summer meals; to the 
     Committee on Education and the Workforce.
       127. Also, a memorial of the General Assembly of the State 
     of New Jersey, relative to Assembly Resolution No. 234, 
     urging Congress not eliminate the property tax deduction 
     allowable under United States federal tax law; to the 
     Committee on Ways and Means.
