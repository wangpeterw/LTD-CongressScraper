     STRENGTHENING STATE AND LOCAL CYBER CRIME FIGHTING ACT OF 2017

  The SPEAKER pro tempore. The unfinished business is the vote on the 
motion to suspend the rules and pass the bill (H.R. 1616) to amend the 
Homeland Security Act of 2002 to authorize the National Computer 
Forensics Institute, and for other purposes, as amended, on which the 
yeas and nays were ordered.
  The Clerk read the title of the bill.
  The SPEAKER pro tempore. The question is on the motion offered by the 
gentleman from Virginia (Mr. Goodlatte) that the House suspend the 
rules and pass the bill, as amended.
  The vote was taken by electronic device, and there were--yeas 408, 
nays 3, not voting 19, as follows:
