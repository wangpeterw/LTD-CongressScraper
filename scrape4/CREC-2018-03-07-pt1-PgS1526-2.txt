                  AMELIA EARHART POST OFFICE BUILDING

   The bill (S. 2040) to designate the facility of the United States 
Postal Service located at 621 Kansas Avenue in Atchison, Kansas, as the 
``Amelia Earhart Post Office Building,'' was ordered to be engrossed 
for a third reading and was read the third time.
