                         MESSAGE FROM THE HOUSE

  At 10:06 a.m., a message from the House of Representatives, delivered 
by Mrs. Cole, one of its reading clerks, announced that pursuant to 36 
U.S.C. 2302, and the order of the House of January 3, 2017, the Speaker 
appoints the following Members of the House of Representatives to the 
United States Holocaust Memorial Council: Mr. Deutch of Florida and Mr. 
Schneider of Illinois.
