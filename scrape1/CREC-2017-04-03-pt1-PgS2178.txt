                    AUTHORITY FOR COMMITTEES TO MEET

  Mr. FLAKE. Mr. President, I have one request for a committee to meet 
during today's session of the Senate. It has the approval of the

Senate, the following committee is authorized to meet during today's 
session of the Senate:


                       COMMITTEE ON THE JUDICIARY

  The Committee on the Judiciary is authorized to meet during the 
session of the Senate, on April 3, 2017, at 10 a.m., in SH-216 of the 
Hart Senate Office Building.
  The PRESIDING OFFICER. The majority leader.
