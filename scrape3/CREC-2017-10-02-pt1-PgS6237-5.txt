       MOMENT OF SILENCE FOR THE VICTIMS OF THE LAS VEGAS ATTACK

  Mr. McCONNELL. Mr. President, I ask unanimous consent that the Senate 
now observe a moment of silence for the victims of the Las Vegas 
attack.
  The PRESIDENT pro tempore. Without objection, it is so ordered.
  The Senate will now observe a moment of silence for the victims of 
the attack in Las Vegas.
  (Moment of silence.)
  The PRESIDING OFFICER (Mr. Young). The majority leader is recognized.
