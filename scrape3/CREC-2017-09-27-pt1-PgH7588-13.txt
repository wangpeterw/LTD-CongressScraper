            By Ms. ROSEN:
        H.R. 3855.
        Congress has the power to enact this legislation pursuant 
     to the following:
       Article I, Section 8, Clauses 14 and 16 of the U.S. 
     Constitution (``To make Rules for the Government and 
     Regulation of the land and naval Forces'' and ``To provide 
     for organizing, arming, and disciplining, the Militia, and 
     for governing such Part of them as may be employed in the 
     Service of the United States, reserving to the States 
     respectively, the Appointment of the Officers, and the 
     Authority of training the Militia according to the discipline 
     prescribed by Congress)''.
