                           ORDER OF PROCEDURE

  Mr. McCONNELL. Mr. President, I ask unanimous consent that following 
leader remarks on Friday, April 7, and notwithstanding rule XXII, there 
be 2 hours of debate equally divided in the usual form; further, that 
upon the use or yielding back of time, the Senate vote on the Gorsuch 
nomination with no intervening action or debate.
  The PRESIDING OFFICER. Without objection, it is so ordered.
