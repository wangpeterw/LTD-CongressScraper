             SMALL BANK HOLDING COMPANY RELIEF ACT OF 2018

  The SPEAKER pro tempore. Pursuant to clause 8 of rule XX, the 
unfinished business is the vote on passage of the bill (H.R. 4771) to 
raise the consolidated assets threshold under the small bank holding 
company policy statement, and for other purposes, on which the yeas and 
nays were ordered.
  The Clerk read the title of the bill.
  The SPEAKER pro tempore. The question is on the passage of the bill.
  The vote was taken by electronic device, and there were--yeas 280, 
nays 139, not voting 11, as follows:
