                MEASURE PLACED ON THE CALENDAR--S. 2192

  Mr. GRASSLEY. Mr. President, I understand that there is a bill at the 
desk that is due for a second reading.
  The PRESIDING OFFICER. The clerk will read the bill by title for the 
second time.
  The legislative clerk read as follows:

       A bill (S. 2192) to strengthen border security, increase 
     resources for enforcement of immigration laws, and for other 
     purposes.

  Mr. GRASSLEY. In order to place the bill on the calendar under the 
provisions of rule XIV, I object to further proceedings.
  The PRESIDING OFFICER. Objection having been heard, the bill will be 
placed on the calendar.
