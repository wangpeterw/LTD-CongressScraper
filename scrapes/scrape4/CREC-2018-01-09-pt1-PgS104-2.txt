                        MESSAGES FROM THE HOUSE

  At 10:04 a.m., a message from the House of Representatives, delivered 
by Mrs. Cole, one of its reading clerks, announced that the House has 
agreed to H. Res. 577, resolving that the Clerk of the House inform the 
Senate that a quorum of the House is present and that the House is 
ready to proceed with business.


                          Enrolled Bill Signed

  At 4:09 p.m., a message from the House of Representatives, delivered 
by Mrs. Cole, one of its reading clerks, announced that the Speaker has 
signed the following enrolled bill:

       H.R. 3759. An act to provide for the establishment and 
     maintenance of a Family Caregiving Strategy, and for other 
     purposes.

  The enrolled bill was subsequently signed by the President pro 
tempore (Mr. Hatch).
