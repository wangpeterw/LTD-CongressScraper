      RELATING TO SELECTION OF MEMBERS TO SERVE ON INVESTIGATIVE 
                SUBCOMMITTEES OF THE COMMITTEE ON ETHICS

  The SPEAKER pro tempore (Mr. Yoder). Without objection, the Speaker 
or a designee and the minority leader or a designee shall each name a 
total of 15 Members, Delegates, or the Resident Commissioner from the 
respective party of such individual who are not members of the 
Committee on Ethics to be available to serve on investigative 
subcommittees of that committee during the 115th Congress, pursuant to 
clause 5(a)(4) of rule X.
  There was no objection.
