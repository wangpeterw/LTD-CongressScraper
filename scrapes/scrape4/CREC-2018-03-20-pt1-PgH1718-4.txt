 PERMISSION FOR MEMBER TO BE CONSIDERED AS PRIMARY SPONSOR OF H.R. 756

  Mr. GARRETT. Mr. Speaker, I ask unanimous consent that I may 
hereafter be considered to be the primary sponsor of H.R. 756, a bill 
originally introduced by Representative Chaffetz of Utah, for the 
purpose of adding cosponsors and requesting reprintings pursuant to 
clause 7 of rule XII.
  The SPEAKER pro tempore. Is there objection to the request of the 
gentleman from Virginia?
  There was no objection.
