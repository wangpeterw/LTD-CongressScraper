                NOTICE OF INTENT TO OBJECT TO PROCEEDING

  I, Senator Tammy Duckworth, intend to object to proceeding to the 
nomination of William L. Wehrum, of Delaware, to be an Assistant 
Administrator of the Environmental Protection Agency, dated October 18, 
2017.

nomination of Michael Dourson, of Ohio, to be Assistant Administrator 
for Toxic Substances of the Environmental Protection Agency, dated 
October 18, 2017.
