            By Mr. BUCK:
        H.R. 519.
        Congress has the power to enact thislegislation pursuant 
     to the following:
       ``The Congress shall have Power to lay and collect Taxes, 
     Duties, Imposts, andExcises, to pay the Debts, and provide 
     for the common Defense and General Welfare of the United 
     States; but all Duties andImposts and Excises shall be 
     uniform throughout the United States.''
