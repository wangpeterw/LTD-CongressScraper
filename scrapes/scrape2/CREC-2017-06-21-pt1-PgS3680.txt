                            VOTE EXPLANATION

  Mr. BENNET. Mr. President, on June 5, 2017, the Senate adopted S. 
Res. 176, a resolution commemorating the 50th anniversary of the 
reunification of Jerusalem. I am a cosponsor of this resolution. 
Unfortunately, I missed the vote due to a delayed flight. If I were in 
Washington, DC, during the time of this vote, I would have cast my vote 
in support of this resolution.
