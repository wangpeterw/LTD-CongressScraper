              APPOINTMENT OF ACTING PRESIDENT PRO TEMPORE

  ThePRESIDING OFFICER. The clerk will please read a communication to 
the Senate from the President pro tempore (Mr.Hatch).
  The legislative clerk read the following letter:

                                                      U.S. Senate,


                                        President pro tempore,

                                  Washington,DC, January 13, 2017.
     To the Senate:
       Under the provisions of rule I, paragraph 3, of the 
     StandingRules of the Senate, I hereby appoint the Honorable 
     Tim Scott, a Senator from the State of South Carolina, to 
     perform theduties of the Chair.
                                                   Orrin G. Hatch,
                                            President pro tempore.

  Mr. SCOTT thereupon assumedthe Chair as Acting President pro tempore.
