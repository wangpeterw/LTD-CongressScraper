                    BILL PRESENTED TO THE PRESIDENT

  Karen L. Haas, Clerk of the House, reported that on September 11, 
2017, she presented to the President of the United States, for his 
approval, the following bill:

       H.R. 3732. To amend section 1113 of the Social Security Act 
     to provide authority for increased fiscal year 2017 and 2018 
     payments for temporary assistance to United States citizens 
     returned from foreign countries.
