               SR. CHIEF RYAN OWENS POST OFFICE BUILDING

  The bill (H.R. 3109) to designate the facility of the United States 
Postal Service located at 1114 North 2nd Street in Chillicothe, 
Illinois, as the ``Sr. Chief Ryan Owens Post Office Building,'' was 
ordered to a third reading and was read the third time.
