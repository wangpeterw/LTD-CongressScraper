 REPORT ON RESOLUTION PROVIDING FOR CONSIDERATION OF S. 140, AMENDING 
THE WHITE MOUNTAIN APACHE TRIBE WATER RIGHTS QUANTIFICATION ACT OF 2010

  Mr. SESSIONS, from the Committee on Rules, submitted a privileged 
report (Rept. No. 115-503) on the resolution (H. Res. 681) providing 
for consideration of the bill (S. 140) to amend the White Mountain 
Apache Tribe Water Rights Quantification Act of 2010 to clarify the use 
of amounts in the WMAT Settlement Fund, which was referred to the House 
Calendar and ordered to be printed.
