                           EXECUTIVE SESSION

                                 ______
                                 

                           EXECUTIVE CALENDAR

  Mr. McCONNELL. Mr. President, I move to proceed to executive session 
to consider Calendar No. 153, Russell Vought.
  The PRESIDING OFFICER. The question is on agreeing to the motion.
  The motion was agreed to.
  The PRESIDING OFFICER. The clerk will report the nomination.
  The senior assistant legislative clerk read the nomination of Russell 
Vought, of Virginia, to be Deputy Director of the Office of Management 
and Budget.


                             Cloture Motion

  Mr. McCONNELL. Mr. President, I send a cloture motion to the desk.
  The PRESIDING OFFICER. The cloture motion having been presented under 
rule XXII, the Chair directs the clerk to read the motion.
  The senior assistant legislative clerk read as follows:

                             Cloture Motion

       We, the undersigned Senators, in accordance with the 
     provisions of rule XXII of the Standing Rules of the Senate, 
     do hereby move to bring to a close debate on the nomination 
     of Russell Vought, of Virginia, to be Deputy Director of the 
     Office of Management and Budget.
         Mitch McConnell, Pat Roberts, Roy Blunt, Shelley Moore 
           Capito, Thom Tillis, Richard Burr, Roger F. Wicker, 
           Mike Crapo, Orrin G. Hatch, John Barrasso, Johnny 
           Isakson, Michael B. Enzi, John Boozman, Mike Rounds, 
           James M. Inhofe, John Thune, Lindsey Graham.
