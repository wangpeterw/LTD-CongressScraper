                         MESSAGE FROM THE HOUSE

  At 10:48 a.m., a message from the House of Representatives, delivered 
by Mr. Novotny, one of its reading clerks, announced that the House has 
passed the following bill, in which it requests the concurrence of the 
Senate:

       H.R. 2581. An act to amend the Internal Revenue Code of 
     1986 to require the provision of social security numbers as a 
     condition of receiving the health insurance premium tax 
     credit.
