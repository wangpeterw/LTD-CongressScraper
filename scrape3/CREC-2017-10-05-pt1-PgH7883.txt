 HOUR OF MEETING ON TOMORROW, AND ADJOURNMENT FROM FRIDAY, OCTOBER 6, 
                   2017, TO TUESDAY, OCTOBER 10, 2017

  Mr. McCARTHY. Mr. Speaker, I ask unanimous consent that when the 
House adjourns today, it adjourn to meet at 10:30 a.m. tomorrow; and 
further, when the House adjourns on that day, it adjourn to meet on 
Tuesday next, when it shall convene at noon for morning-hour debate and 
2 p.m. for legislative business.
  The SPEAKER pro tempore (Mr. Hollingsworth). Is there objection to 
the request of the gentleman from California?
  There was no objection.
