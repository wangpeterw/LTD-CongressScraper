                        MESSAGE FROM THE SENATE

  A message from the Senate by Mrs. Curtis, one of its clerks, 
announced that the Senate has passed without amendment a bill of the 
House of the following title:

       H.R. 3819. An act to amend title 38, United States Code, to 
     extend certain expiring provisions of law administered by the 
     Secretary of Veterans Affairs, and for other purposes.
