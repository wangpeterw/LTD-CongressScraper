                   EXECUTIVE AND OTHER COMMUNICATIONS

  The following communications were laid before the Senate, together 
with accompanying papers, reports, and documents, and were referred as 
indicated:

        EC-2443. A communication from the Director, Administrative 
     Office of the United States Courts, transmitting, pursuant to 
     law, a report relative to compliance by the United States 
     courts of appeals and district courts with the time 
     limitations established for deciding habeas corpus death 
     penalty petitions; to the Committee on the Judiciary.
        EC-2444. A communication from the Federal Liaison Officer,

     President of the Senate on July 21, 2017; to the Committee on 
     the Judiciary.
