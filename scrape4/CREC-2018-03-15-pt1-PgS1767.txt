                    AMENDMENTS SUBMITTED AND PROPOSED

       SA 2210. Mr. McCONNELL (for Mr. Johnson (for himself and 
     Mrs. McCaskill)) proposed an amendment to the bill H.R. 3210, 
     to require the Director of the National Background 
     Investigations Bureau to submit a report on the backlog of 
     personnel security clearance investigations, and for other 
     purposes.
       SA 2211. Mr. McCONNELL (for Mr. Grassley) proposed an 
     amendment to the bill S. 1869, to reauthorize and rename the 
     position of Whistleblower Ombudsman to be the Whistleblower 
     Protection Coordinator.
