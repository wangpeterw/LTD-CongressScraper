                KENNEDY-KING NATIONAL COMMEMORATIVE SITE

  Mr. CURTIS (during the Special Order of Ms. Jackson Lee). Madam 
Speaker, I ask unanimous consent to take from the Speaker's table the 
bill (H.R. 4851) to establish the Kennedy-King National Historic Site 
in the State of Indiana, and for other purposes, with the Senate 
amendment thereto, and concur in the Senate amendment.
  The Clerk read the title of the bill.
  The SPEAKER pro tempore. The Clerk will report the Senate amendment.
  The Clerk read as follows:
  Senate amendment:

       In section 3, strike subsection (d).

  The SPEAKER pro tempore. Is there objection to the request of the 
gentleman from Utah?
  There was no objection.
  A motion to reconsider was laid on the table.
