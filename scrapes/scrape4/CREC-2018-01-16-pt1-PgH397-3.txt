 RESIGNATIONS AS MEMBER OF COMMITTEE ON SCIENCE, SPACE, AND TECHNOLOGY 
                   AND COMMITTEE ON NATURAL RESOURCES

  The SPEAKER pro tempore laid before the House the following 
resignations as a member of the Committee on Science, Space, and 
Technology and the Committee on Natural Resources:

                                    Congress of the United States,


                                     House of Representatives,

                                 Washington, DC, January 16, 2018.
     Hon. Speaker of the House,
     House of Representatives,
     Washington, DC.
       Dear Speaker Ryan: Due to my election to the Committee on 
     Ways and Means, this letter is to inform you that I am 
     resigning my seats on the House Science, Space and Technology 
     Committee and the House Natural Resources Committee.
           Sincerely,
                                                     Darin LaHood,
                                               Member of Congress.

  The SPEAKER pro tempore. Without objection, the resignations are 
accepted.
  There was no objection.
