                    BILLS PRESENTED TO THE PRESIDENT

  Karen L. Haas, Clerk of the House, reported that on May 25, 2017, she 
presented to the President of the United States, for his approval, the 
following bills:

       H.R. 375. To designate the Federal building and United 
     States courthouse located at 719 Church Street in Nashville, 
     Tennessee, as the ``Fred D. Thompson Federal Building and 
     United States Courthouse''.
       H.R. 366. To amend the Homeland Security Act of 2002 to 
     direct the Under Secretary for Management of the Department 
     of Homeland Security to make certain improvements in managing 
     the Department's vehicle fleet, and for other purposes.
