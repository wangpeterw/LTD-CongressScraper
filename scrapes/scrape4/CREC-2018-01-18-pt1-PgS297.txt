                            VOTE EXPLANATION

  Mr. ROUNDS. Madam President, on December 21, 2017, Senator Isakson 
was necessarily absent for the vote on the continuing resolution. He 
intended to vote yea, and it was my intention to vote no. As a courtesy 
to Senator Isakson, I voted yea, in order to pair our votes.
