STRENGTHENING PROTECTIONS FOR SOCIAL SECURITY BENEFICIARIES ACT OF 2018

  The SPEAKER pro tempore. The unfinished business is the vote on the 
motion to suspend the rules and pass the bill (H.R. 4547) to amend 
titles II, VIII, and XVI of the Social Security Act to improve and 
strengthen the representative payment program, as amended, on which the 
yeas and nays were ordered.
  The Clerk read the title of the bill.
  The SPEAKER pro tempore. The question is on the motion offered by the 
gentleman from Texas (Mr. Brady) that the House suspend the rules and 
pass the bill, as amended.
  The vote was taken by electronic device, and there were--yeas 396, 
nays 0, not voting 34, as follows:
