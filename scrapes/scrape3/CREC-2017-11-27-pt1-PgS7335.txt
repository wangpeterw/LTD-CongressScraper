            UNANIMOUS CONSENT AGREEMENT--EXECUTIVE CALENDAR

  Mr. McCONNELL. Mr. President, I ask unanimous consent, 
notwithstanding rule XXII, that at 4 p.m. on Tuesday, November 28, 
there be 90 minutes of postcloture debate remaining on the Katsas 
nomination, equally divided between the leaders or their designees.
  The PRESIDING OFFICER. Without objection, it is so ordered.
