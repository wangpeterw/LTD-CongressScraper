REPORT ON RESOLUTION PROVIDING FOR CONSIDERATION OF H.R. 4790, VOLCKER 
 RULE REGULATORY HARMONIZATION ACT, AND PROVIDING FOR CONSIDERATION OF 
                        MOTIONS TO SUSPEND RULES

  Mr. SESSIONS, from the Committee on Rules, submitted a privileged 
report (Rept. No. 115-629) on the resolution (H. Res. 811) providing 
for consideration of the bill (H.R. 4790) to amend the Volcker rule to 
give the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System sole 
rulemaking authority, to exclude

[[Page H3081]]

community banks from the requirements of the Volcker rule, and for 
other purposes, and providing for consideration of motions to suspend 
the rules, which was referred to the House Calendar and ordered to be 
printed.
