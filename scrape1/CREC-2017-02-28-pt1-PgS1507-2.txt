                           ORDER OF PROCEDURE

  Mr. McCONNELL. Mr. President, I ask unanimous consent that following 
leader remarks on Wednesday, March 1, there be 20 minutes of debate, 
equally divided, prior to the confirmation vote on Executive Calendar 
No. 8, Ryan Zinke to be Secretary of the Interior, followed by up to 10 
minutes of debate, equally divided, prior to the cloture vote on 
Executive Calendar No. 5, the nomination of Ben Carson to be Secretary 
of Housing and Urban Development, and if cloture is invoked, time be 
counted as if invoked at 1 a.m. that day.
  The PRESIDING OFFICER. Is there objection?
  Without objection, it is so ordered.
