                    BILLS PRESENTED TO THE PRESIDENT

  Karen L. Haas, Clerk of the House, reported that on January 20, 2017, 
she presented to the President of the United States, for his approval, 
the following bill:

       H.R. 39. To amend title 5, United States Code, to codify 
     the Presidential Innovation Fellows Program, and for other 
     purposes.

  Karen L. Haas, Clerk of the House, further reported that on January 
23, 2017, she presented to the President of the United States, for his 
approval, the following bill:

       H.R. 72. To ensure the Government Accountability Office has 
     adequate access to information.
