                           MEASURES REFERRED

  The following bills were read the first and the second times by 
unanimous consent, and referred as indicated:

       H.R. 469. An act to impose certain limitations on consent 
     decrees and settlement agreements by agencies that require 
     the agencies to take regulatory action in accordance with the 
     terms thereof, and for other purposes; to the Committee on 
     the Judiciary.
       H.R. 3342. An act to impose sanctions on foreign persons 
     that are responsible for gross violations of internationally 
     recognized human rights by reason of the use by Hizballah of 
     civilians as human shields, and for other purposes; to the 
     Committee on Foreign Relations.
