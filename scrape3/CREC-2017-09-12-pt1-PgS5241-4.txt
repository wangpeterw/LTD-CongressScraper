                         DISCHARGED NOMINATION

  The Senate Committee on Armed Services was discharged from further 
consideration of the following nomination pursuant to S. Res. 470 of 
the 113th Congress and the nomination was referred sequentially to the 
Committee on Homeland Security and Governmental Affairs for 20 calendar 
days under authority of the order of the Senate of 01/07/2009:

       ROBERT P. STORCH, OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, TO BE 
     INSPECTOR GENERAL OF THE NATIONAL SECURITY AGENCY.
