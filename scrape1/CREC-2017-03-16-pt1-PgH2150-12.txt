            By Mr. SCHIFF:
        H.R. 1584.
        Congress has the power to enact this legislation pursuant 
     to the following:
       The Orca Responsibility Care and Advancement Act is 
     constitutionally authorized under Article I, Section 8, 
     Clause 3, ``the Commerce Clause'' and Article I, Section 8, 
     Clause 18, ``the Necessary and Proper Clause.'' Additionally, 
     the Preamble to the Constitution provides support of the 
     authority to enact legislation to promote the General 
     Welfare.
