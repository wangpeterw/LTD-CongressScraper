                 ORDERS FOR THURSDAY, OCTOBER 19, 2017

  Mr. GARDNER. Mr. President, I ask unanimous consent that when the 
Senate completes its business today, it adjourn until 9:30 a.m., 
Thursday, October 19; further, that following the prayer and pledge, 
the morning hour be deemed expired, the Journal of proceedings be 
approved to date, the time for the two leaders be reserved for their 
use later in the day, and morning business be closed; finally, that 
following leader remarks, the Senate resume consideration of H. Con. 
Res. 71 under the previous order.
  The PRESIDING OFFICER. Without objection, it is so ordered.
