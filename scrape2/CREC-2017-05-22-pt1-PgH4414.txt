REPORT ON RESOLUTION PROVIDING FOR CONSIDERATION OF H.R. 953, REDUCING 
                     REGULATORY BURDENS ACT OF 2017

  Mr. WOODALL, from the Committee on Rules, submitted a privileged 
report (Rept. No. 115-145) on the resolution (H. Res. 348) providing 
for consideration of the bill (H.R. 953) to amend the Federal 
Insecticide, Fungicide, and Rodenticide Act and the Federal Water 
Pollution Control Act to clarify Congressional intent regarding the 
regulation of the use of pesticides in or near navigable waters, and 
for other purposes, which was referred to the House Calendar and 
ordered to be printed.
