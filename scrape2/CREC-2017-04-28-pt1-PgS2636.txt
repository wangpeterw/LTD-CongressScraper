              INTRODUCTION OF BILLS AND JOINT RESOLUTIONS

  The following bills and joint resolutions were introduced, read the 
first and second times by unanimous consent, and referred as indicated:

           By Mr. BLUNT (for himself and Mr. Reed):
       S. 989. A bill to amend the Public Health Service Act to 
     provide for the participation of pediatric subspecialists in 
     the National Health Service Corps program, and for other 
     purposes; to the Committee on Health, Education, Labor, and 
     Pensions.
           By Mr. MORAN (for himself, Mr. Udall, Mr. Daines, and 
             Mr. Warner):
       S. 990. A bill to modernize Government information 
     technology, and for other purposes; to the Committee on 
     Homeland Security and Governmental Affairs.
