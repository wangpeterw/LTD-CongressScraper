                            PETITIONS, ETC.

       66. The SPEAKER presented a petition of the Common Council

     No. 34-R 2017, urging Senator Charles Schumer, Senator

     necessary actions to ensure that the SALT Deduction remains a 
     part of the Federal Tax Code; which was referred to the 
     Committee on Ways and Means.
