    REPORT ON RESOLUTION PROVIDING FOR CONSIDERATION OF H.R. 1039, 
                PROBATION OFFICER PROTECTION ACT OF 2017

  Mr. COLLINS of Georgia, from the Committee on Rules, submitted a 
privileged report (Rept. No. 115-127) on the resolution (H. Res. 324) 
providing for consideration of the bill (H.R. 1039) to amend section 
3606 of title 18, United States Code, to grant probation officers 
authority to arrest hostile third parties who obstruct or impede a 
probation officer in the performance of official duties, which was 
referred to the House Calendar and ordered to be printed.
