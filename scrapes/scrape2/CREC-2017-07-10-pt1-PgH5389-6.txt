                COMMUNICATION FROM THE DEMOCRATIC LEADER

  The SPEAKER pro tempore laid before the House the following 
communication from the Honorable Nancy Pelosi, Democratic Leader:

                                                     July 6, 2017.
     Hon. Paul D. Ryan,
     Speaker of the House of Representatives,
     Washington, DC.
       Dear Speaker Ryan: Pursuant to Section 431(a)(3) of the 
     Consolidated Appropriations Act of 2017 (Pub. L. 115-31), I 
     am pleased to appoint the following individuals to serve as 
     Commissioners to the Women's Suffrage Centennial Commission:
       Ms. Nicola Miner of San Francisco, California
       Ms. Jennifer Siebel Newsom of San Francisco, California
       Thank you for your attention to these recommendations.
           Sincerely,
                                                     Nancy Pelosi,
     Democratic Leader.
