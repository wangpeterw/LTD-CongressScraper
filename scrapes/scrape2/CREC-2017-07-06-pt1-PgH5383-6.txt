                COMMUNICATION FROM THE DEMOCRATIC LEADER

  The SPEAKER pro tempore laid before the House the following 
communication from the Honorable Nancy Pelosi, Democratic Leader:

                                                    June 30, 2017.
     Hon. Paul D. Ryan,
     Speaker of the House,
     Washington, DC.
       Dear Mr. Speaker: Pursuant to the National Foundation on 
     the Arts and the Humanities Act of 1965 (20 U.S.C. 955(b)), I 
     am pleased to reappoint The Honorable Chellie Pingree of 
     Maine to serve on the National Council on the Arts.
       Thank you for your attention to this appointment.
           Sincerely,
                                                     Nancy Pelosi,
     Democratic Leader.
