                           ORDER OF PROCEDURE

  Mr. McCONNELL. Mr. President, I ask unanimous consent that 
notwithstanding rule XXII, at 11 a.m. on Thursday, November 9, there be 
30 minutes of postcloture time remaining on the Wehrum nomination, 
equally divided between the leaders or their designees, and that 
following the use or yielding back of that time, the Senate vote on the 
confirmation of the Wehrum nomination; that if confirmed, the motion to 
reconsider be considered made and laid upon the table and the President 
be immediately notified of the Senate's action; further, that following 
disposition of the Wehrum nomination, the Senate stand in recess until 
1:45 p.m., and that at 1:45 p.m., the Senate vote on the motion to 
invoke cloture on the Kan nomination with no intervening action or 
debate.
  The PRESIDING OFFICER. Without objection, it is so ordered.
