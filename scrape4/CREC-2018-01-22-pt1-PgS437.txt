        PROVIDING FOR A CORRECTION IN THE ENROLLMENT OF H.R. 195

  Mr. McCONNELL. Madam President, I ask unanimous consent that the 
Senate proceed to the immediate consideration of S. Con. Res. 33.
  The PRESIDING OFFICER. The clerk will report the concurrent 
resolution by title.
  The legislative clerk read as follows:

       A concurrent resolution (S. Con. Res. 33) providing for a 
     correction in the enrollment of H.R. 195.

  There being no objection, the Senate proceeded to consider the 
concurrent resolution.
  Mr. McCONNELL. I ask unanimous consent that the resolution be agreed 
to and the motion to reconsider be considered made and laid upon the 
table with no intervening action or debate.
  The PRESIDING OFFICER. Without objection, it is so ordered.
  The concurrent resolution (S. Con. Res. 33) was agreed to.
  (The concurrent resolution is printed in today's Record under 
``Submitted Resolutions.'')
