 MOTION TO CLOSE CONFERENCE COMMITTEE MEETINGS ON H.R. 2810, NATIONAL 
             DEFENSE AUTHORIZATION ACT FOR FISCAL YEAR 2018

  Mr. THORNBERRY. Mr. Speaker, pursuant to clause 12 of rule XXII, I 
move that meetings of the conference between the House and the Senate 
on H.R. 2810 may be closed to the public at such times as classified 
national security information may be discussed, provided that any 
sitting Member of Congress shall be entitled to attend any meeting of 
the conference.
  The SPEAKER pro tempore. Pursuant to clause 12 of rule XXII, the 
motion is not debatable, and the yeas and nays are ordered.
  This is a 5-minute vote.
  The vote was taken by electronic device, and there were--yeas 414, 
nays 8, not voting 11, as follows:
