                         MESSAGE FROM THE HOUSE


                          Enrolled Bill Signed

  At 2:14 p.m., a message from the House of Representatives, delivered 
by Mrs. Cole, one of its reading clerks, announced that the Speaker pro 
tempore (Mr. Harris) has signed the following enrolled bill:

       H.R. 525. An act to encourage visits between the United 
     States and Taiwan at all levels, and for other purposes.

  The enrolled bill was subsequently signed by the President pro 
tempore (Mr. Hatch).
