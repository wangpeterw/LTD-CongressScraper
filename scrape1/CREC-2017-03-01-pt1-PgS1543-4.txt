                           ORDER OF PROCEDURE

  Mr. McCONNELL. Mr. President, I ask unanimous consent that following 
leader remarks on Thursday, March 2, there be 20 minutes of debate, 
equally divided, prior to the confirmation vote on Executive Calendar 
No. 5, the nomination of Ben Carson to be Secretary of Housing and 
Urban Development, followed by up to 10 minutes of debate, equally 
divided, prior to the cloture vote on Executive Calendar No. 9, the 
nomination of Rick Perry to be Secretary of Energy, and if cloture is 
invoked, time be counted as if invoked at 7 a.m. that day.
  The PRESIDING OFFICER. Without objection, it is so ordered.
