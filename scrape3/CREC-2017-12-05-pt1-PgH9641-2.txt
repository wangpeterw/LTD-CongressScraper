 MODIFICATION IN APPOINTMENT OF CONFEREES ON H.R. 1, TAX CUTS AND JOBS 
                                  ACT

  The SPEAKER pro tempore. Without objection and pursuant to clause 11 
of rule I, the Chair removes the gentleman from Oregon (Mr. Walden) as 
a conferee on H.R. 1 and appoints the gentleman from Michigan (Mr. 
Upton) to fill the vacancy.
  There was no objection.
  The SPEAKER pro tempore. The Clerk will notify the Senate of the 
change in conferees.
