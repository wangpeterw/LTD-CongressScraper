                          REPORTING AUTHORITY

  Ms. MURKOWSKI. Mr. President, I ask unanimous consent that 
notwithstanding the Senate's adjournment, committees be authorized to 
report legislative and executive matters on Friday, August 18, from 10 
a.m. to 12 p.m.
  The PRESIDING OFFICER. Without objection, it is so ordered.
  Ms. MURKOWSKI. Mr. President, I suggest the absence of a quorum.
  The PRESIDING OFFICER. The clerk will call the roll.
  The senior assistant legislative clerk proceeded to call the roll.
  Ms. MURKOWSKI. Mr. President, I ask unanimous consent that the order 
for the quorum call be rescinded.
  The PRESIDING OFFICER. Without objection, it is so ordered.
