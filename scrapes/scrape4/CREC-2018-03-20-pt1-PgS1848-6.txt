                              WITHDRAWALS

  Executive Message transmitted by the President to the Senate on March 
20, 2018 withdrawing from further Senate consideration the following 
nominations:

       STEPHEN AKARD, OF INDIANA, TO BE DIRECTOR GENERAL OF THE 
     FOREIGN SERVICE, VICE ARNOLD A. CHACON, RESIGNED, WHICH WAS 
     SENT TO THE SENATE ON JANUARY 8, 2018.
       JOHNATHAN MILLER, OF VIRGINIA, TO BE AN ASSISTANT 
     ADMINISTRATOR OF THE UNITED STATES AGENCY FOR INTERNATIONAL 
     DEVELOPMENT, VICE LINDA I. ETIM, WHICH WAS SENT TO THE SENATE 
     ON JANUARY 18, 2018.
