    CONGRESSIONAL EARMARKS, LIMITED TAX BENEFITS, OR LIMITED TARIFF 
                                BENEFITS

  Under clause 9 of rule XXI, lists or statements on congressional 
earmarks, limited tax benefits, or limited tariff benefits were 
submitted as follows:

       The amendment to be offered by Chairman Goodlatte, or a 
     designee, to H.R. 985, the Fairness in Class Action 
     Litigation and Furthering Asbestos Claim Transparency Act,

     benefits, or limited tariff benefits as defined in clause 9 
     of rule XXI.
