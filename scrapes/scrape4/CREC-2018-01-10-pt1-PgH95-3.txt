                          Personal Explanation

  Mr. LARSON of Connecticut. Mr. Speaker, on Wednesday, January 10, 
2018, I missed rollcall votes 5-7 on the motions to adjourn from Mr. 
Espaillat, Mr. Gutierrez, and Mr. Grijalva. I was attending meetings 
off-campus and was not able to return for these unexpected votes that 
were not on the House schedule. If I had been present for these votes, 
I would have voted: ``Nay'' on rollcall vote 5 on the motion to adjourn 
from Mr. Espaillat, ``Nay'' on rollcall vote 6 on the motion to adjourn 
from Mr. Gutierrez, and ``Nay'' on rollcall vote 7 on the motion to 
adjourn from Mr. Grijalva.
