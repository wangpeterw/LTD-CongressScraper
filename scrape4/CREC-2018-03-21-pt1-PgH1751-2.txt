  PERMISSION TO POSTPONE PROCEEDINGS ON MOTIONS TO SUSPEND THE RULES 
          THROUGH LEGISLATIVE DAY OF THURSDAY, MARCH 22, 2018

  Mr. GOWDY. Mr. Speaker, I ask unanimous consent that, notwithstanding 
clause 8 of rule XX, further proceedings on the questions of agreeing 
to motions to suspend the rules with regard to H.R. 4227, H.R. 4467, 
H.R. 5089, and H.R. 5131, may continue to be postponed through the 
legislative day of Thursday, March 22, 2018.
  The SPEAKER pro tempore. Is there objection to the request of the 
gentleman from South Carolina?
  There was no objection.
