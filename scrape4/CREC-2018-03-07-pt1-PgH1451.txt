  PERMISSION FOR MEMBER TO BE CONSIDERED AS FIRST SPONSOR OF H.R. 676

  Mr. ELLISON. Mr. Speaker, I ask unanimous consent that I may 
hereafter be considered to be the first sponsor of H.R. 676, the 
Expanded & Improved Medicare For All Act, a bill originally introduced 
by Representative John Conyers from Michigan, for the purposes of 
adding cosponsors and requesting reprintings pursuant to clause 7 of 
rule XII.
  The SPEAKER pro tempore. Is there objection to the request of the 
gentleman from Minnesota?
  There was no objection.
