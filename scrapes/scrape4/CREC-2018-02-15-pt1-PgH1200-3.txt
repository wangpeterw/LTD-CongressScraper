   APPOINTMENT AS INSPECTOR GENERAL FOR U.S. HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES

  The SPEAKER pro tempore. The Chair announces, on behalf of the 
Speaker, majority leader and minority leader, their joint appointment, 
pursuant to clause 6 of rule II, and the order of the House of January 
3, 2017, of Mr. Michael Ptasienski, McLean, Virginia, as Inspector 
General for the U.S. House of Representatives.
