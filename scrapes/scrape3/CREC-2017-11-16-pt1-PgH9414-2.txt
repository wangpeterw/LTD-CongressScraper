               SR. CHIEF RYAN OWENS POST OFFICE BUILDING

  The SPEAKER pro tempore (Mr. Dunn). The unfinished business is the 
question on suspending the rules and passing the bill (H.R. 3109) to 
designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 
1114 North 2nd Street in Chillicothe, Illinois, as the ``Sr. Chief Ryan 
Owens Post Office Building''.
  The Clerk read the title of the bill.
  The SPEAKER pro tempore. The question is on the motion offered by the 
gentleman from Kentucky (Mr. Comer) that the House suspend the rules 
and pass the bill, H.R. 3109.
  The question was taken; and (two-thirds being in the affirmative) the 
rules were suspended and the bill was passed.
  A motion to reconsider was laid on the table.
