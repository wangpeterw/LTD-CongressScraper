           By Mr. KEATING:
       H.R. 5244.
       Congress has the power to enact this legislation pursuant 
     to the following:
       Article I, Section 8 of the United States Constitution, 
     which provides Congress with the power to regulate commerces 
     and relations between the United States and Indian Tribes, 
     and to pass all laws necessary and proper for carrying into 
     execution of the foregoing powers, as well as all other power 
     vested by the Constitution.
