  REPORT ON H.R. 3219, DEPARTMENT OF DEFENSE APPROPRIATIONS ACT, 2018

  Ms. GRANGER, from the Committee on Appropriations, submitted a 
privileged report (Rept. No. 115-219) on the bill (H.R. 3219) making 
appropriations for the Department of Defense for the fiscal year ending 
September 30, 2018, and for other purposes, which was referred to the 
Union Calendar and ordered to be printed.
  The SPEAKER pro tempore (Mr. Katko). Pursuant to clause 1, rule XXI, 
all points of order are reserved on the bill.
