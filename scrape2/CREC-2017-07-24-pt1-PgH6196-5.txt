         DEPARTMENT OF VETERANS AFFAIRS BONUS TRANSPARENCY ACT

  The SPEAKER pro tempore. The unfinished business is the vote on the 
motion to suspend the rules and pass the bill (S. 114) to amend title 
38, United States Code, to require the Secretary of Veterans Affairs to 
submit an annual report regarding performance awards and bonuses 
awarded to certain

[[Page H6197]]

high-level employees of the Department of Veterans Affairs, as amended, 
on which the yeas and nays were ordered.
  The Clerk read the title of the bill.
  The SPEAKER pro tempore. The question is on the motion offered by the 
gentleman from Tennessee (Mr. Roe) that the House suspend the rules and 
pass the bill, as amended.
  This is a 5-minute vote.
  The vote was taken by electronic device, and there were--yeas 219, 
nays 186, not voting 28, as follows:
