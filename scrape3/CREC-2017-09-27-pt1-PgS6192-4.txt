                ORDERS FOR THURSDAY, SEPTEMBER 28, 2017

  Mr. INHOFE. Mr. President, I ask unanimous consent that when the 
Senate completes its business today, it adjourn until 9:30 a.m., 
Thursday, September 28; further, that following the prayer and pledge, 
the morning hour be deemed expired, the Journal of proceedings be 
approved to date, the time for the two leaders be reserved for their 
use later in the day, and morning business be closed; finally, that 
following leader remarks, the Senate proceed to executive session and 
resume consideration of the Erickson nomination, with the time until 
the cloture vote equally divided between the two leaders or their 
designees.
  The PRESIDING OFFICER. Without objection, it is so ordered.
