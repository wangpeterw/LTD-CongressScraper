                     EXECUTIVE REPORTS OF COMMITTEE

       Joel M. Carson III, of New Mexico, to be United States 
     Circuit Judge for the Tenth Circuit.
       Colm F. Connolly, of Delaware, to be United States District 
     Judge for the District of Delaware.

     Judge for the Middle District of Florida.

       William M. McSwain, of Pennsylvania, to be United States

       Johnny Lee Kuhlman, of Oklahoma, to be United States

     four years.
       Joseph D. McClain, of Indiana, to be United States Marshal 
     for the Southern District of Indiana for the term of four 
     years.
       David A. Weaver, of Colorado, to be United States Marshal 
     for the District of Colorado for the term of four years.
