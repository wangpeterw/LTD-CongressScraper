                            VOTE EXPLANATION

  Mr. DONNELLY. Mr. President, yesterday, September 27, 2017, I was in 
Indiana with the President of the United States and was unable to vote. 
Had I been present, I would have voted in support of Mr. Heath 
Tarbert's nomination to be an Assistant Secretary of the Treasury and 
in support of Mr. Makan Delrahim's nomination to be an Assistant 
Attorney General.
