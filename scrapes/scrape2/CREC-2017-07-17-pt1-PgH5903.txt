  REPORT ON RESOLUTION PROVIDING FOR CONSIDERATION OF H.R. 806, OZONE 
                  STANDARDS IMPLEMENTATION ACT OF 2017

  Mr. BURGESS, from the Committee on Rules, submitted a privileged 
report (Rept. No. 115-229) on the resolution (H. Res. 451) providing 
for consideration of the bill (H.R. 806) to facilitate efficient State 
implementation of ground-level ozone standards, and for other purposes, 
which was referred to the House Calendar and ordered to be printed.
