                COMMUNICATION FROM THE DEMOCRATIC LEADER

  The SPEAKER pro tempore laid before the House the following 
communication from the Honorable Nancy Pelosi, Democratic Leader:

                                                    March 1, 2018.
     Hon. Paul D. Ryan,
     Speaker of the House of Representatives, U.S. Capitol, 
         Washington, DC.
       Dear Speaker Ryan: Pursuant to section 30442(b)(3) of the 
     Bipartisan Budget Act of 2018 (Pub. L. 115-123), I am pleased 
     to announce the joint appointment, together with the Minority 
     Leader of the Senate, of the Honorable Nita Lowey of New York 
     to serve as co-chair of the Joint Select Committee on Budget 
     and Appropriations Process Reform.
       Thank you for your attention to this appointment.
           Sincerely,
                                                     Nancy Pelosi,
     Democratic Leader.
