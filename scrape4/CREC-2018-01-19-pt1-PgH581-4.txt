                    BILLS PRESENTED TO THE PRESIDENT

  Karen L. Haas, Clerk of the House, reported that on January 17, 2018, 
she presented to the President of the United States, for his approval, 
the following bills:

       H.R. 984. To extend Federal recognition to the Chickahominy 
     Indian Tribe, the Chickahominy Indian Tribe-Eastern Division, 
     the Upper Mattaponi Tribe, the Rappahannock Tribe, Inc., the 
     Monacan Indian Nation, and the Nansemond Indian Tribe.
       H.R. 4641. To authorize the President to award the Medal of 
     Honor to John L. Canley for acts of valor during the Vietnam 
     War while a member of the Marine Corps.
