                    BILL PRESENTED TO THE PRESIDENT

  Karen L. Haas, Clerk of the House, reported that on February 6, 2018, 
she presented to the President of the United States, for his approval, 
the following bill:

       H.R. 4708. To amend the Homeland Security Act of 2002 to 
     require the Secretary of Homeland Security to issue 
     Department of Homeland Security-wide guidance and develop 
     training programs as part of the Department of Homeland 
     Security Blue Campaign, and for other purposes.
