                        ENROLLED BILL PRESENTED

  The Secretary of the Senate reported that on today, June 15, 2017, 
she had presented to the President of the United States the following 
enrolled bill:

       S. 1083. An act to amend section 1214 of title 5, United 
     States Code, to provide for stays during a period that the 
     Merit Systems Protection Board lacks a quorum.
