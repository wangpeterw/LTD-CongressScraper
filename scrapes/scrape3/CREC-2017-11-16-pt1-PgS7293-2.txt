                            VOTE EXPLANATION

 Mr. BOOKER. Mr. President, I was necessarily absent for the 
votes on confirmation of Executive Calendar No. 300, the motion to 
invoke cloture on Executive Calendar No. 313, the motion to invoke 
cloture on Executive Calendar No. 314, and the confirmation of 
Executive Calendar No. 313.
  On vote No. 277, had I been present, I would have voted nay on the 
confirmation of Executive Calendar No. 300.
  On vote No. 278, had I been present, I would have voted yea on the 
motion to invoke cloture on Executive Calendar No. 313.
  On Vote No. 279, had I been present, I would have voted yea on the 
motion to invoke cloture on Executive Calendar No. 314.
  On Vote No. 280, had I been present, I would have voted yea on the 
confirmation of Executive Calendar No. 313.
