REMOVAL OF CONFEREE AND APPOINTMENT OF CONFEREE ON H.R. 2810, NATIONAL 
             DEFENSE AUTHORIZATION ACT FOR FISCAL YEAR 2018

  The SPEAKER pro tempore. Without objection and pursuant to clause 11 
of rule I, the Chair removes the gentleman from Oregon (Mr. Walden) as 
a conferee on H.R. 2810 and appoints the gentleman from Illinois (Mr. 
Shimkus) to fill the vacancy.
  There was no objection.
  The SPEAKER pro tempore. The Clerk will notify the Senate of the 
change in conferees.
