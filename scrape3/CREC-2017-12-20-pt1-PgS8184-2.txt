                              THE CALENDAR

  Mr. McCONNELL. Mr. President, I ask unanimous consent that the Senate 
proceed to the immediate consideration of the following bills en bloc: 
Calendar No. 124, S. 117 and Calendar No. 56, S. 501. I further ask 
unanimous consent that, where applicable, the committee-reported 
amendment be agreed to, the bills, as amended, if amended, be 
considered read a third time and passed, and the motions to reconsider 
be considered made and laid upon the table, all en bloc.
  The PRESIDING OFFICER. Without objection, it is so ordered.
