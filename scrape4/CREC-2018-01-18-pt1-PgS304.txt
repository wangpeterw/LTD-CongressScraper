    FEDERAL REGISTER PRINTING SAVINGS ACT OF 2017--MOTION TO PROCEED

  Mr. McCONNELL. Madam President, I understand the Senate has received 
a message from the House to accompany H.R. 195.
  The PRESIDING OFFICER. The Senator is correct.
  Mr. McCONNELL. Madam President, I move that the Chair lay before the 
Senate the message to accompany H.R. 195 and ask for the yeas and nays 
on my motion.
  The PRESIDING OFFICER. Is there a sufficient second?
  There appears to be a sufficient second.
  The question is on agreeing to the motion.
  The clerk will call the roll.
  The senior assistant legislative clerk called the roll.
  Mr. CORNYN. The following Senator is necessarily absent: the Senator 
from Arizona (Mr. McCain).
  The PRESIDING OFFICER (Mr. Hoeven). Are there any other Senators in 
the Chamber desiring to vote?
  The result was announced--yeas 97, nays 2, as follows:
