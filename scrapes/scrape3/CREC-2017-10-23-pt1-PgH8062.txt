                   C-TPAT REAUTHORIZATION ACT OF 2017

  The SPEAKER pro tempore. The unfinished business is the vote on the 
motion to suspend the rules and pass the bill (H.R. 3551) to amend the 
Security and Accountability for Every Port Act of 2006 to reauthorize 
the Customs-Trade Partnership Against Terrorism Program, and for other 
purposes, as amended, on which the yeas and nays were ordered.
  The Clerk read the title of the bill.
  The SPEAKER pro tempore. The question is on the motion offered by the 
gentlewoman from Arizona (Ms. McSally) that the House suspend the rules 
and pass the bill, as amended.
  The vote was taken by electronic device, and there were--yeas 402, 
nays 1, not voting 29, as follows:
