            By Mr. SERRANO:
        H.R. 2508.
        Congress has the power to enact this legislation pursuant 
     to the following:
       Article I, Section 8, Clause 4, which states that Congress 
     shall have the power ``to Establish a uniform Rule of 
     Naturalization,'' and Article I, Section 8, Clause 3, which 
     states that Congress shall have the power ``to regulate 
     Commerce with foreign Nations.''
