                    FURTHER MESSAGE FROM THE SENATE

  A further message from the Senate by Ms. Curtis, one of its clerks, 
announced that the Senate concur in the House amendment to the Senate 
amendment to the bill (H.R. 244) ``An Act to encourage effective, 
voluntary investments to recruit, employ, and retain men and women who 
have served in the United States military with annual Federal awards to 
employers recognizing such efforts, and for other purposes.''.
  The message also announced that the Senate has agreed to without 
amendment a concurrent resolution of the House of the following title:

       H. Con. Res. 53. Concurrent resolution providing for a 
     correction in the enrollment of H.R. 244.
