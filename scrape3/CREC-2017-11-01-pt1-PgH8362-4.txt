 PERMISSION FOR MEMBER TO BE CONSIDERED AS FIRST SPONSOR OF H.RES. 428

  Mr. COSTELLO of Pennsylvania. Mr. Speaker, I ask unanimous consent 
that I may hereafter be considered as the primary sponsor of H. Res. 
428, a resolution originally introduced by Representative Murphy of 
Pennsylvania, for the purpose of adding cosponsors and requesting 
reprintings under clause 7 of rule XII.
  The SPEAKER pro tempore. Is there objection to the request of the 
gentleman from Pennsylvania?
  There was no objection.
