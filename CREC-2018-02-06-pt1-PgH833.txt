PROVIDING FOR CONSIDERATION OF SENATE AMENDMENT TO H.R. 1892, HONORING 
                          HOMETOWN HEROES ACT

  The SPEAKER pro tempore. The unfinished business is the vote on 
ordering the previous question on the resolution (H. Res. 727) 
providing for consideration of the Senate amendment to the bill (H.R. 
1892) to amend title 4, United States Code, to provide for the flying 
of the flag at half-staff in the event of the death of a first 
responder in the line of duty, on which the yeas and nays were ordered.
  The Clerk read the title of the resolution.
  The SPEAKER pro tempore. The question is on ordering the previous 
question.
  This will be a 5-minute vote.
  The vote was taken by electronic device, and there were--yeas 235, 
nays 189, not voting 6, as follows
