                   BORINQUENEERS POST OFFICE BUILDING

  The bill (H.R. 4042) to designate the facility of the United States 
Postal Service located at 1415 West Oak Street, in Kissimmee, Florida, 
as the ``Borinqueneers Post Office Building,'' was ordered to a third 
reading and was read the third time.
