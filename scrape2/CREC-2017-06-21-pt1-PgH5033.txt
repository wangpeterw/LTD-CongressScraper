    REPORT ON RESOLUTION PROVIDING FOR CONSIDERATION OF H.R. 2842, 
  ACCELERATING INDIVIDUALS INTO THE WORKFORCE ACT, AND PROVIDING FOR 
             CONSIDERATION OF MOTIONS TO SUSPEND THE RULES

  Mr. COLE, from the Committee on Rules, submitted a privileged report 
(Rept. No. 115-187) on the resolution (H. Res. 396) providing for 
consideration of the bill (H.R. 2842) to provide for the conduct of 
demonstration projects to test the effectiveness of subsidized 
employment for TANF recipients, and providing for consideration of 
motions to suspend the rules, which was referred to the House Calendar 
and ordered to be printed.
