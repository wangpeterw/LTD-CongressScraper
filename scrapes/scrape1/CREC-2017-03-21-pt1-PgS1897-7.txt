                  ORDERS FOR WEDNESDAY, MARCH 22, 2017

  Mr. SULLIVAN. Mr. President, I ask unanimous consent that when the 
Senate completes its business today, it adjourn until 10:30 a.m., 
Wednesday, March 22; further, that following the prayer and pledge, the 
morning hour be deemed expired, the Journal of proceedings be approved 
to date, the time for the two leaders be reserved for their use later 
in the day, and morning business be closed.

[[Page S1898]]

  The PRESIDING OFFICER. Without objection, it is so ordered.
