           MESSAGE FROM THE HOUSE RECEIVED DURING ADJOURNMENT


                         Enrolled Bills Signed

  Under the authority of the order of the Senate of January 3, 2017, 
the Secretary of the Senate, on March 16, 2018, during the adjournment 
of the Senate, received a message from the House of Representatives 
announcing that the Speaker had signed the following enrolled bills:

       S. 188. An act to amend title 31, United States Code, to 
     prohibit the use of Federal funds for the costs of painting 
     portraits of officers and employees of the Federal 
     Government, and for other purposes.
       S. 324. An act to amend title 38, United States Code, to 
     improve the provision of adult day health care services for 
     veterans.
