                COMMUNICATION FROM THE DEMOCRATIC LEADER

  The SPEAKER pro tempore laid before the House the following 
communication from the Honorable Nancy Pelosi, Democratic Leader:

                                                February 20, 2018.
     Hon. Paul D. Ryan,
     Speaker of the House of Representatives,
     Washington, DC.
       Dear Speaker Ryan: Pursuant to section 931 of the 
     Comprehensive Addiction and Recovery Act of 2016, Public Law 
     114-198, I am pleased to appoint the following individuals to 
     serve as Commissioners to the Creating Options for Veterans' 
     Expedited Recovery (COVER) Commission:
       Dr. Michael J. Potoczniak of El Cerrito, California
       Dr. Shira Maguen of San Francisco, California
       Thank you for your attention to these recommendations.
           Sincerely,
                                                     Nancy Pelosi,
     Democratic Leader.
