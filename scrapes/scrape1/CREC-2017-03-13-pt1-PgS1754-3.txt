               WISHING THE STENOGRAPHER A SPEEDY RECOVERY

  Mr. SCHUMER. Mr. President, let me thank the attending physician for 
quickly coming to the aid of one of our stenographers. They all do an 
amazing job. They are the unsung heroes, and we wish the stenographer a 
speedy recovery.
