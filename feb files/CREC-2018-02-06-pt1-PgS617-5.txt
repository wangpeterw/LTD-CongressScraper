                            MORNING BUSINESS

  The PRESIDING OFFICER. Under the previous order, the Senate will be 
in a period of morning business, with Senators permitted to speak 
therein, with the time until 12 noon equally divided between the two 
leaders or their designees.
  If no one yields time, the time will be charged equally.
