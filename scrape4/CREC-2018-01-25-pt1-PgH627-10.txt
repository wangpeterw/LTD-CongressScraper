                         SENATE BILLS REFERRED

  Bills of the Senate of the following titles were taken from the

       S. 1395. An act to revise the boundaries of certain John H. 
     Chafee Coastal Barrier Resources System units in Delaware; to 
     the Committee on Natural Resources.
       S. 2152. An act to amend title 18, United States Code, to 
     provide for assistance for victims of child pornography, and 
     for other purposes; to the Committee on the Judiciary.
