                   ORDERS FOR TUESDAY, JUNE 13, 2017

  Mr. McCONNELL. Mr. President, I ask unanimous consent that when the 
Senate completes its business today, it adjourn until 10 a.m., Tuesday, 
June 13; further, that following the prayer and pledge, the morning 
hour be deemed expired, the Journal of proceedings be approved to date, 
the time for the two leaders be reserved for their use later in the 
day, and morning business be closed; finally, that following leader 
remarks, Senator Paul or his designee be recognized, as under the 
previous order.
  The PRESIDING OFFICER. Without objection, it is so ordered.
