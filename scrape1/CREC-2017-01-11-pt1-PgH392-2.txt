            By Mr. HUFFMAN:
        H.R. 448.
        Congress has the power toenact this legislation pursuant 
     to the following:
       Clause 1, Section 8 of Article 1 of the United States 
     Constitution, whichreads: ``The Congress shall have Power to 
     lay and collect Taxes, Duties, Imposts, and Excises, to pay 
     the Debts, and provide forthe common Defense and General 
     Welfare of the United States; but all Duties and Imposts and 
     Excises shall be uniform throughoutthe United States.''
