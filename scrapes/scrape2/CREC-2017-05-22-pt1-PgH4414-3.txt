                  GLOBAL CHILD PROTECTION ACT OF 2017

  The SPEAKER pro tempore. The unfinished business is the vote on the 
motion to suspend the rules and pass the bill (H.R. 1862) to amend 
title 18, United States Code, to expand the scope of certain 
definitions pertaining to unlawful sexual conduct, and for other 
purposes, on which the yeas and nays were ordered.
  The Clerk read the title of the bill.
  The SPEAKER pro tempore. The question is on the motion offered by the 
gentleman from Virginia (Mr. Goodlatte) that the House suspend the 
rules and pass the bill.
  The vote was taken by electronic device, and there were--yeas 372, 
nays 30, not voting 28, as follows:
