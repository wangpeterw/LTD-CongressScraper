                            PETITIONS, ETC.

  Under clause 3 of rule XII, petitions and papers were laid on the 
clerk's desk and referred as follows:

       15. The SPEAKER presented a petition of the Council of the 
     City of Salem, VA, relative to Resolution 1308, urging Senate 
     Majority Leader Mitch McConnell, Senator Mark Warner, Senator 
     Tim Kaine, and all United States Senators to reintroduce the 
     Marketplace Fairness Act into the United States Senate during 
     its 2017 session; to the Committee on the Judiciary.
       16. Also, a petition of the Board of Supervisors of Roanoke 
     County, VA, relative to Resolution 012417-1, urging 
     Congressional action on the Marketplace Fairness Act or other 
     legislation to collect and remit sales taxes structured on a 
     system of collection based upon the purchaser's location; to 
     the Committee on the Judiciary.
