          BILL AND JOINT RESOLUTION PRESENTED TO THE PRESIDENT

  Karen L. Haas, Clerk of the House, reported that on March 21, 2017, 
she presented to the President of the United States, for his approval, 
the following bill and joint resolution:

       H.R. 1362. To name the Department of Veterans Affairs 
     community-based outpatient clinic in Pago Pago, American 
     Samoa, the Faleomavaega Eni Fa'aua'a Hunkin VA Clinic.
       H.J. Res. 42. Disapproving the rule submitted by the 
     Department of Labor relating to drug testing of unemployment 
     compensation applicants.
