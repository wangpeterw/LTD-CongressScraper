                         MESSAGE FROM THE HOUSE

  At 10:55 a.m., a message from the House of Representatives, delivered 
by Mr. Novotny, one of its reading clerks, announced that the House has 
passed the following bill, in which it requests the concurrence of the 
Senate:

       H.R. 1695. An act to amend title 17, United States Code, to 
     provide additional responsibilities for the Register of 
     Copyrights, and for other purposes.

  The message further announced that pursuant to section 4003(e) of the 
21st Century Cures Act (Public Law 114-255), the Minority Leader 
appoints the following individual on the part of the

[[Page S2615]]

House of Representatives to the Health Information Technology Advisory 
Committee: Dr. Steven Lane of Palo Alto, California.
