            SUBMISSION OF CONCURRENT AND SENATE RESOLUTIONS

  The following concurrent resolutions and Senate resolutions were 
read, and referred (or acted upon), as indicated:

           By Mr. DAINES (for himself, Mr. Hatch, Mr. Lankford, 
             Mr. Risch, Mr. Blunt, Mr. Inhofe, Mr. Cotton, Mr. 
             Crapo, Mr. Lee, Mr. Boozman, Mr. Tillis, and Mrs. 
             Ernst):
       S. Con. Res. 32. A concurrent resolution affirming the 
     importance of religious freedom as a fundamental human right 
     that is essential to a free society and protected for all 
     people of the United States under the Constitution of the 
     United States, and recognizing the 232nd anniversary of the 
     enactment of the Virginia Statute for Religious Freedom; to 
     the Committee on the Judiciary.
