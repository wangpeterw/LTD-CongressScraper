            SUBMISSION OF CONCURRENT AND SENATE RESOLUTIONS

  The following concurrent resolutions and Senate resolutions were 
read, and referred (or acted upon), as indicated:

           By Mr. CRUZ:
       S. Res. 291. A resolution affirming the historical 
     connection of the Jewish people to the ancient and sacred 
     city of Jerusalem and condemning efforts at the United 
     Nations Educational, Scientific, and Cultural Organization 
     (UNESCO) to deny Judaism's millennia-old historical, 
     religious, and cultural ties to Jerusalem; to the Committee 
     on Foreign Relations.

       S. Res. 292. A resolution condemning the brutal and 
     senseless attack at a country music festival in Las Vegas, 
     Nevada, honoring the memory of the victims of the deadly 
     shooting, offering condolences to, and expressing support 
     for, the families and friends of the victims and all of the 
     individuals who were affected by the attack, and applauding 
     the dedication and bravery of law enforcement officers, 
     firefighters, paramedics, emergency medical technicians, 
     medical professionals, hotel security and staff, and 
     community members in responding to the attack; considered and 
     agreed to.
