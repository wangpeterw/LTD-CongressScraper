                    ORDERS FOR MONDAY, JULY 24, 2017

  Mr. McCONNELL. Mr. President, I ask unanimous consent that when the 
Senate completes its business today, it adjourn until 4 p.m., Monday, 
July 24; further, that following the prayer and pledge, the morning 
hour be deemed expired, the Journal of proceedings be approved to date, 
the time for the two leaders be reserved for their use later in the 
day, and morning business be closed; further, that following leader 
remarks, the Senate proceed to executive session and resume 
consideration of the Bernhardt nomination; finally, that the 
postcloture time on the Bernhardt nomination expire at 5:30 p.m., 
Monday, July 24.
  The PRESIDING OFFICER. Without objection, it is so ordered.
