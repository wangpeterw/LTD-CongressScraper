                               WITHDRAWAL

  Executive Message transmitted by the President to the Senate on 
February 27, 2018 withdrawing from further Senate consideration the 
following nomination:

       ROBERT M. WEAVER, OF OKLAHOMA, TO BE DIRECTOR OF THE INDIAN 
     HEALTH SERVICE, DEPARTMENT OF HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES, FOR 
     THE TERM OF FOUR YEARS, VICE YVETTE ROUBIDEAUX, TERM EXPIRED, 
     WHICH WAS SENT TO THE SENATE ON OCTOBER 24, 2017.
