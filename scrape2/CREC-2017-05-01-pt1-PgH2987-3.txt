REPORT ON RESOLUTION PROVIDING FOR CONSIDERATION OF H.R. 1180, WORKING 
FAMILIES FLEXIBILITY ACT OF 2017; PROVIDING FOR PROCEEDINGS DURING THE 
 PERIOD FROM MAY 5, 2017, THROUGH MAY 15, 2017; AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES

  Mr. COLLINS of Georgia, from the Committee on Rules, submitted a 
privileged report (Rept. No. 115-106) on the resolution (H. Res. 299) 
providing for consideration of the bill (H.R. 1180) to amend the Fair 
Labor Standards Act of 1938 to provide compensatory time for employees 
in the private sector; providing for proceedings during the period from 
May 5, 2017, through May 15, 2017; and for other purposes, which was 
referred to the House Calendar and ordered to be printed.
