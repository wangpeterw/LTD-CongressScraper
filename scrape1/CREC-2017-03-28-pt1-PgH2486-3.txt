                ANNOUNCEMENT BY THE SPEAKER PRO TEMPORE

  The SPEAKER pro tempore. Pursuant to clause 8 of rule XX, proceedings 
will resume on questions previously postponed.
  Votes will be taken in the following order:
  Ordering the previous question on House Resolution 229;
  Adoption of House Resolution 229, if ordered;
  Ordering the previous question on House Resolution 230; and
  Adoption of House Resolution 230, if ordered.
  The first electronic vote will be conducted as a 15-minute vote. 
Remaining electronic votes will be conducted as 5-minute votes.
