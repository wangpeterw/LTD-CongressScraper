                   ORDERS FOR THURSDAY, MARCH 9, 2017

  Mr. McCONNELL. Mr. President, I ask unanimous consent that when the 
Senate completes its business today, it adjourn until 10 a.m., 
Thursday, March 9; that following the prayer and pledge, the morning 
hour be deemed expired, the Journal of proceedings be approved to date, 
the time for the two leaders be reserved for their use later in the 
day, and that morning business be closed; finally, that 30 minutes of 
the majority time on H.J. Res. 57 be under the control of Senator Blunt 
or his designee.
  The PRESIDING OFFICER. Without objection, it is so ordered.
