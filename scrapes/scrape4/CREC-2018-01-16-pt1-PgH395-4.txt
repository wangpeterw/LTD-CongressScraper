                 MISCELLANEOUS TARIFF BILL ACT OF 2018

  The SPEAKER pro tempore. The unfinished business is the vote on the 
motion to suspend the rules and pass the bill (H.R. 4318) to amend the 
Harmonized Tariff Schedule of the United States to modify temporarily 
certain rates of duty, as amended, on which the yeas and nays were 
ordered.
  The Clerk read the title of the bill.
  The SPEAKER pro tempore. The question is on the motion offered by the 
gentleman from Michigan (Mr. Bishop) that the House suspend the rules 
and pass the bill, as amended.
  The vote was taken by electronic device, and there were--yeas 402, 
nays 0, not voting 28, as follows:
