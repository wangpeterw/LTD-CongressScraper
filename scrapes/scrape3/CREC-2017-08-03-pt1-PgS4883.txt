                    AMENDMENTS SUBMITTED AND PROPOSED

       SA 752. Mr. ALEXANDER (for Mr. Manchin) proposed an 
     amendment to the bill S. 581, to include information 
     concerning a patient's opioid addiction in certain medical 
     records.
