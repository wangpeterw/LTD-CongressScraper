 PERMISSION FOR MEMBER TO BE CONSIDERED AS PRIMARY SPONSOR OF H.R. 40 
                             AND H.R. 1498

  Ms. JACKSON LEE. Mr. Speaker, I ask unanimous consent that I may 
hereafter be considered to be the primary sponsor of H.R. 40, the 
Commission to Study and Develop Reparation Proposals for African-
Americans Act, and H.R. 1498, the End Racial Profiling Act, bills 
originally introduced by Representative Conyers of Michigan, for the 
purposes of adding cosponsors and requesting reprintings pursuant to 
clause 7 of rule XII.
  The SPEAKER pro tempore. Is there objection to the request of the 
gentlewoman from Texas?
  There was no objection.
