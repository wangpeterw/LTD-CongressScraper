                    ADMINISTRATION OF OATH OF OFFICE

  The PRESIDENT pro tempore. If the Senator-designate will now present 
himself at the desk, the Chair will administer the oath of office.
  The Senator-designee, Luther Strange, escorted by Mr. Sessions and 
Mr. Shelby, advanced to the desk of the Vice President; the oath 
prescribed by law was administered to him by the President pro tempore; 
and he subscribed to the oath in the Official Oath Book.
  The PRESIDENT pro tempore. Congratulations, Senator.
  (Applause, Senators rising.)
