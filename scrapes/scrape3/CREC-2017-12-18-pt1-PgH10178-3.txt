 REPORT ON RESOLUTION PROVIDING FOR CONSIDERATION OF CONFERENCE REPORT 
 ON H.R. 1, TAX CUTS AND JOBS ACT; PROVIDING FOR CONSIDERATION OF H.R. 
3312, SYSTEMIC RISK DESIGNATION IMPROVEMENT ACT OF 2017; AND FOR OTHER 
                                PURPOSES

  Mr. SESSIONS, from the Committee on Rules, submitted a privileged 
report (Rept. No. 115-474) on the resolution (H. Res. 667) providing 
for consideration of the conference report to accompany the bill (H.R. 
1) to provide for reconciliation pursuant to titles II and V of the 
concurrent resolution on the budget for fiscal year 2018; providing for 
consideration of the bill (H.R. 3312) to amend the Dodd-Frank Wall 
Street Reform and Consumer Protection Act to specify when bank holding 
companies may be subject to certain enhanced supervision, and for other 
purposes; and for other purposes, which was referred to the House 
Calendar and ordered to be printed.
