                 IMPROVING ACCESS TO MATERNITY CARE ACT

  The SPEAKER pro tempore. The unfinished business is the vote on the 
motion to suspend the rules and pass the bill (H.R. 315) to amend the 
Public Health Service Act to distribute maternity care health 
professionals to health professional shortage areas identified as in 
need of maternity care health services, on which the yeas and nays were 
ordered.
  The Clerk read the title of the bill.
  The SPEAKER pro tempore. The question is on the motion offered by the 
gentleman from Texas (Mr. Burgess) that the House suspend the rules and 
pass the bill.
  The vote was taken by electronic device, and there were--yeas 405, 
nays 0, not voting 28, as follows:
