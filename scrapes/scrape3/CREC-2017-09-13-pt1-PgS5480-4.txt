              INTRODUCTION OF BILLS AND JOINT RESOLUTIONS

  The following bills and joint resolutions were introduced, read the 
first and second times by unanimous consent, and referred as indicated:

           By Mr. GARDNER:
       S. 1802. A bill to amend title 5, United States Code, to

     officer; to the Committee on Homeland Security and 
     Governmental Affairs.
           By Mr. HATCH (for himself, Mr. Schatz, Mr. Gardner, Mr. 
             Coons, and Mr. Tillis):
       S. 1803. A bill to improve medical research on marijuana; 
     to the Committee on the Judiciary.
           By Mr. SANDERS (for himself, Ms. Baldwin, Mr. 
             Blumenthal, Mr. Booker, Mr. Franken, Mrs. Gillibrand, 
             Ms. Harris, Mr. Heinrich, Ms. Hirono, Mr. Leahy, Mr. 
             Markey, Mr. Merkley, Mr. Schatz, Mrs. Shaheen, Mr. 
             Udall, Ms. Warren, and Mr. Whitehouse):
       S. 1804. A bill to establish a Medicare-for-all health 
     insurance program; to the Committee on Finance.
