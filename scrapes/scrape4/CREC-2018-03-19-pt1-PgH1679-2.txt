              KENNEDY-KING NATIONAL COMMEMORATIVE SITE ACT

  The SPEAKER. Without objection, 5-minute voting will continue.
  There was no objection.
  The SPEAKER. The unfinished business is the vote on the motion to 
suspend the rules and pass the bill (H.R. 4851) to establish the 
Kennedy-King National Historic Site in the State of Indiana, and for 
other purposes, as amended, on which the yeas and nays were ordered.
  The Clerk read the title of the bill.
  The SPEAKER. The question is on the motion offered by the gentleman 
from Colorado (Mr. Lamborn) that the House suspend the rules and pass 
the bill, as amended.
  This is a 5-minute vote.
  The vote was taken by electronic device, and there were--yeas 391, 
nays 0, not voting 38, as follows:
