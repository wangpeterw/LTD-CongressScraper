                ANNOUNCEMENT BY THE SPEAKER PRO TEMPORE

  The SPEAKER pro tempore (Mr. Duncan of Tennessee). Pursuant to clause 
8 of rule XX, the Chair will postpone further proceedings today on 
motions to suspend the rules on which a recorded vote or the yeas and 
nays are ordered, or on which the vote incurs objection under clause 6 
of rule XX.
  Record votes on postponed questions will be taken later.
