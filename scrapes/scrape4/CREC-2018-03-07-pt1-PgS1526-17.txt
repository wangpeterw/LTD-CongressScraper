                 WALTER S. McAFEE POST OFFICE BUILDING

  The bill (H.R. 3655) to designate the facility of the United States 
Postal Service located at 1300 Main Street in Belmar, New Jersey, as 
the ``Dr. Walter S. McAfee Post Office Building,'' was ordered to a 
third reading and was read the third time.
