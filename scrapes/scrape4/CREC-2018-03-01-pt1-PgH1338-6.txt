                              ADJOURNMENT

  Mr. BUDD. Mr. Speaker, I move that the House do now adjourn.
  The motion was agreed to; accordingly (at 1 o'clock and 36 minutes 
p.m.), under its previous order and pursuant to House Resolution 759, 
the House adjourned until Monday, March 5, 2018, at noon for morning-
hour debate, as a mark of respect to the memory of the late Billy 
Graham.
