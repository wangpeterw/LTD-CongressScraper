OUR PURPOSE

TRANSPARENCY

Congress appears to be a "black-box" of sorts nowadays. With the especially heated political climate throughout the past few years, it's critical that there be transparency between the work thats being done in Congress and the average citizen.

DAILY RECORD

There are public, government-provided online records of almost every word spoken by representatives, witnesses, and senators on a daily basis. However, to make use of this information, one has navigate through many pages, find a specific days' discourse to view, and then sift through thousands of lines of a single .txt file to find any information of use.

TEDIOUS

To the average citizen and especially researchers interested in this dataset, this is far too time-consuming and inaccessible to yield any meaningful results or analysis of what's actually going on behind the scenes in Congress.

OUR SOLUTION

In response, Searchlight, a liberating textual data project sponsored by Goodly Labs, created an accessible database of information for citizens and researchers alike. Now, anyone can easily query and sift through our scraped and parsed congressional data and receive it in a csv format.

USER-FRIENDLY

Search for speeches by a given represenative or senator, or maybe by party. Maybe particular month or date is of interest to you, or maybe speeches given by delegates from a certain state. Whatever your query, our project helps you collect information that best matches your needs.
