     REPORT ON RESOLUTION PROVIDING FOR CONSIDERATION OF H.R. 849, 
           PROTECTING SENIORS' ACCESS TO MEDICARE ACT OF 2017

  Mr. BURGESS, from the Committee on Rules, submitted a privileged 
report (Rept. No. 115-381) on the resolution (H. Res. 600) providing 
for consideration of the bill (H.R. 849) to repeal the provisions of 
the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act providing for the 
Independent Payment Advisory Board, which was referred to the House 
Calendar and ordered to be printed.
