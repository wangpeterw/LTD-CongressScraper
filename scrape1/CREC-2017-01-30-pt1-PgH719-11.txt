       DUNGENESS CRAB FISHERY MANAGEMENT SUNSET PROVISION REMOVAL

  The SPEAKER pro tempore. The unfinished business is the vote on the 
motion to suspend the rules and pass the bill (H.R. 374) to remove the 
sunset provision of section 203 of Public Law 105-384, and for other 
purposes, on which the yeas and nays were ordered.
  The Clerk read the title of the bill.
  The SPEAKER pro tempore. The question is on the motion offered by the 
gentleman from California (Mr. McClintock) that the House suspend the 
rules and pass the bill.
  The vote was taken by electronic device, and there were--yeas 388, 
nays 0, not voting 44, as follows:
