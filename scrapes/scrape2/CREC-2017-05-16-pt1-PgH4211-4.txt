REPORT ON RESOLUTION PROVIDING FOR CONSIDERATION OF H.R. 115, THIN BLUE 
                                LINE ACT

  Mr. COLLINS of Georgia, from the Committee on Rules, submitted a 
privileged report (Rept. No. 115-126) on the resolution (H. Res. 323) 
providing for consideration of the bill (H.R. 115) to amend title 18, 
United States Code, to provide additional aggravating factors for the 
imposition of the death penalty based on the status of the victim, 
which was referred to the House Calendar and ordered to be printed.
