              JOINT RESOLUTIONS PRESENTED TO THE PRESIDENT

  Karen L. Haas, Clerk of the House, reported that on February 06, 
2017, she presented to the President of the United States, for his 
approval, the following joint resolutions:

       H.J. Res. 41. Providing for congressional disapproval under 
     chapter 8 of title 5, United States Code, of a rule submitted 
     by the Securities and Exchange Commission relating to 
     ``Disclosure of Payments by Resource Extraction Issuers''.
       H.J. Res. 38. Disapproving the rule submitted by the 
     Department of the Interior known as the Stream Protection 
     Rule.
