    REPORT ON RESOLUTION PROVIDING FOR CONSIDERATION OF H.R. 1119, 
 SATISFYING ENERGY NEEDS AND SAVING THE ENVIRONMENT ACT, AND PROVIDING 
 FOR CONSIDERATION OF H.R. 1917, BLOCKING REGULATORY INTERFERENCE FROM 
                       CLOSING KILNS ACT OF 2017

  Ms. CHENEY, from the Committee on Rules, submitted a privileged 
report (Rept. No. 115-586) on the resolution (H. Res. 762) providing 
for consideration of the bill (H.R. 1119) to establish the bases by 
which the Administrator of the Environmental Protection Agency shall 
issue, implement, and enforce certain emission limitations and 
allocations for existing electric utility steam generating units that 
convert coal refuse into energy, and providing for consideration of the 
bill (H.R. 1917) to allow for judicial review of any final rule 
addressing national emission standards for hazardous air pollutants for 
brick and structural clay products or for clay ceramics manufacturing 
before requiring compliance with such rule, which was referred to the 
House Calendar and ordered to be printed.
