 HOUR OF MEETING ON TOMORROW, AND ADJOURNMENT FROM FRIDAY, OCTOBER 27, 
                   2017, TO TUESDAY, OCTOBER 31, 2017

  Mr. McCARTHY. Mr. Speaker, I ask unanimous consent that when the 
House adjourns today, it adjourn to meet at 10 a.m. tomorrow, and 
further, when the House adjourns on that day, it adjourn to meet on 
Tuesday, October 31, 2017, when it shall convene at noon for morning-
hour debate and 2 p.m. for legislative business.
  The SPEAKER pro tempore (Mr. Taylor). Is there objection to the 
request of the gentleman from California?
  There was no objection.
