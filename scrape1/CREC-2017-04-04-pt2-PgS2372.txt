                   ORDERS FOR THURSDAY, APRIL 6, 2017

  Mr. GARDNER. Mr. President, I ask unanimous consent that when the 
Senate completes its business today, it recess until 10 a.m., Thursday, 
April 6; further, that following the prayer and pledge, the time for 
the two leaders be reserved for their use later in the day; further, 
that following leader remarks, the time until the cloture vote on the 
Gorsuch nomination be equally divided between Senators Grassley and 
Feinstein or their designees; finally, that the mandatory quorum call 
with respect to the cloture vote be waived.
  The PRESIDING OFFICER. Without objection, it is so ordered.
