AUTHORIZING REVEREND BILLY GRAHAM TO LIE IN HONOR IN THE ROTUNDA OF THE 
                                CAPITOL

  Mr. WALKER. Mr. Speaker, I ask unanimous consent that the Committee 
on House Administration be discharged from further consideration of 
House Concurrent Resolution 107, and ask for its immediate 
consideration in the House.
  The Clerk read the title of the concurrent resolution.
  The SPEAKER pro tempore. Is there objection to the request of the 
gentleman from North Carolina?
  There was no objection.
  The text of the concurrent resolution is as follows

                            H. Con. Res. 107

       Resolved by the House of Representatives (the Senate 
     concurring),

     SECTION 1. AUTHORIZING REVEREND BILLY GRAHAM TO LIE IN HONOR 
                   IN ROTUNDA OF CAPITOL.

       (a) In General.--Reverend Billy Graham shall be permitted 
     to lie in honor in the rotunda of the Capitol from February 
     28, 2018, to March 1, 2018.
       (b) Implementation.--The Architect of the Capitol, under 
     the direction and supervision of the Speaker of the House of 
     Representatives and the President pro tempore of the Senate, 
     shall take the necessary steps to implement subsection (a).

  The concurrent resolution was agreed to.
  A motion to reconsider was laid on the table.
