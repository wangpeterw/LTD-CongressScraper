                  ORDERS FOR THURSDAY, OCTOBER 5, 2017

  Mr. CRAPO. Mr. President, I ask unanimous consent that when the 
Senate completes its business today, it adjourn until 9:30 a.m., 
Thursday, October 5; further, that following the prayer and pledge, the 
morning hour be deemed expired, the Journal of proceedings be approved 
to date, the time for the two leaders be reserved for their use later 
in the day, and morning business be closed; finally, that following 
leader remarks, the Senate proceed to executive session and resume 
consideration of the Quarles nomination, with the time until 10 a.m. 
equally divided between the two leaders or their designees.
  The PRESIDING OFFICER. Without objection, it is so ordered.
