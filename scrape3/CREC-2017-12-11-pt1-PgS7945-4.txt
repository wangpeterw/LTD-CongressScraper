                         REPORTS OF COMMITTEES

  The following reports of committees were submitted:

       By Mr. THUNE, from the Committee on Commerce, Science, and 
     Transportation, with an amendment in the nature of a 
     substitute:
       S. 875. A bill to require the Comptroller General of the 
     United States to conduct a study and submit a report on 
     filing requirements under the Universal Service Fund programs 
     (Rept. No. 115-192).
       S. 1322. A bill to establish the American Fisheries 
     Advisory Committee to assist in the awarding of fisheries 
     research and development grants, and for other purposes 
     (Rept. No. 115-193).
       By Mr. CORKER, from the Committee on Foreign Relations, 
     with an amendment and with an amended preamble:
       S. Res. 139. A resolution condemning the Government of 
     Iran's state-sponsored persecution of its Baha'i minority and 
     its continued violation of the International Covenants on 
     Human Rights.
       By Mr. CORKER, from the Committee on Foreign Relations, 
     with an amendment in the nature of a substitute:
       S. 1118. A bill to reauthorize the North Korea Human Rights 
     Act of 2004, and for other purposes.
