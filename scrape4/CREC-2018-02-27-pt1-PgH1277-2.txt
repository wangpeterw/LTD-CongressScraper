   ADJOURNMENT FROM THURSDAY, MARCH 1, 2018, TO MONDAY, MARCH 5, 2018

  Mr. COLLINS of Georgia. Mr. Speaker, I ask unanimous consent that 
when the House adjourns on Thursday, March 1, 2018, it adjourn to meet 
on Monday, March 5, 2018, when it shall convene at noon for morning-
hour debate and 2 p.m. for legislative business.
  The SPEAKER pro tempore. Is there objection to the request of the 
gentleman from Georgia?
  There was no objection.
