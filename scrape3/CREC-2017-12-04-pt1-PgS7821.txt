DEPARTMENT OF STATE AUTHORITIES ACT, FISCAL YEAR 2017, IMPROVEMENTS ACT

  Mr. McCONNELL. Mr. President, I ask that the Chair lay before the 
Senate the message to accompany S. 371.
  The Presiding Officer laid before the Senate the following message 
from the House of Representatives:

       Resolved, That the bill from the Senate (S. 371) entitled 
     ``An Act to make technical changes and other improvements to 
     the Department of State Authorities Act, Fiscal Year 2017.'', 
     do pass with an amendment.

  Mr. McCONNELL. I move to concur in the House amendment, and I ask 
unanimous consent that the motion be agreed to and the motion to 
reconsider be considered made and laid upon the table.
  The PRESIDING OFFICER. Without objection, it is so ordered.
