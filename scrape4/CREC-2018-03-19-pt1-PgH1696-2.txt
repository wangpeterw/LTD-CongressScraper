                    BILL PRESENTED TO THE PRESIDENT

  Karen L. Haas, Clerk of the House, reported that on March 15, 2018, 
she presented to the President of the United States, for his approval, 
the following bill.

       H.R. 1177. To direct the Secretary of Agriculture to 
     release on behalf of the United States the condition that 
     certain lands conveyed to the City of Old Town, Maine, be 
     used for a municipal airport, and for other purposes.
