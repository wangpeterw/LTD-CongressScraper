    REPORT ON RESOLUTION PROVIDING FOR CONSIDERATION OF H.R. 1301, 
             DEPARTMENT OF DEFENSE APPROPRIATIONS ACT, 2017

  Ms. CHENEY, from the Committee on Rules, submitted a privileged 
report (Rept. No. 115-26) on the resolution (H. Res. 174) providing for 
consideration of the bill (H.R. 1301) making appropriations for the 
Department of Defense for the fiscal year ending September 30, 2017, 
and for other purposes, which was referred to the House Calendar and 
ordered to be printed.
