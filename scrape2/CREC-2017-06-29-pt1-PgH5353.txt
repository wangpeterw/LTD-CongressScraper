                        MESSAGE FROM THE SENATE

  A message from the Senate by Ms. Curtis, one of its clerks, announced 
that the Secretary of the Senate be directed to request the House to 
return to the Senate the bill (S. 722) ``An Act to provide 
congressional review and to counter Iranian and Russian governments' 
aggression.''.
