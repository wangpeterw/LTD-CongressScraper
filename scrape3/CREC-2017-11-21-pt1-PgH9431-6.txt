                COMMUNICATION FROM THE DEMOCRATIC LEADER

  The SPEAKER pro tempore laid before the House the following 
communication from the Honorable Nancy Pelosi, Democratic Leader:

                                                November 21, 2017.
     Hon. Paul D. Ryan,
     Speaker of the House of Representatives, U.S. Capitol, 
         Washington, DC.
       Dear Speaker Ryan: Pursuant to Section 4(b) of the World 
     War I Centennial Commission Act (Pub. L. 112-272), I am 
     pleased to appoint Ms. Maria Zoe Dunning of San Francisco, 
     California to the World War I Centennial Commission. Ms. 
     Maria Zoe Dunning would replace Mr. Robert Dalessandro, my 
     previous recommendation to the commission, appointed in 2014 
     and who has resigned from the commission.
       Thank you for your attention to this recommendation.
           Sincerely,
                                                     Nancy Pelosi,
     Democratic Leader.
