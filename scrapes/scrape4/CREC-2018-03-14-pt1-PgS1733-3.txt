                           MEASURES REFERRED

  The following bills were read the first and the second times by 
unanimous consent, and referred as indicated:

       H.R. 1800. An act to direct the Secretary of Agriculture to 
     transfer certain Federal land to facilitate scientific 
     research supporting Federal space and defense programs; to 
     the Committee on Agriculture, Nutrition, and Forestry.
       H.R. 4266. An act to clarify the boundary of Acadia 
     National Park, and for other purposes; to the Committee on 
     Energy and Natural Resources.
       H.R. 4465. An act to maintain annual base funding for the 
     Upper Colorado and San Juan fish recovery programs through 
     fiscal year 2023, to require a report on the implementation 
     of those programs, and for other purposes; to the Committee 
     on Energy and Natural Resources.
