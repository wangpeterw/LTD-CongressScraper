                         MESSAGE FROM THE HOUSE

  At 4:05 p.m., a message from the House of Representatives, delivered 
by Mr. Novotny, one of its reading clerks, announced that the House has 
passed the following bill, in which it requests the concurrence of the 
Senate:

       H.R. 2201. An act to amend the Securities Act of 1933 to 
     exempt certain micro-offerings from the registration 
     requirements of such Act, and for other purposes.
