                           EXECUTIVE CALENDAR

  The PRESIDING OFFICER. Under the previous order, the Senate will 
proceed to executive session to consider the following nomination, 
which the clerk will report.
  The bill clerk read the nomination of Jennifer Gillian Newstead, of 
New York, to be Legal Adviser of the Department of State.
  Mr. SCHUMER. Mr. President, I ask for the yeas and nays, and I yield 
back all time.
  The PRESIDING OFFICER. Is there objection?
  Without objection, all time is yielded back.
  Is there a sufficient second?
  There appears to be a sufficient second.
  The question is, Will the Senate advise and consent to the Newstead 
nomination?
  The clerk will call the roll.
  The bill clerk called the roll.
  Mr. CORNYN. The following Senator is necessarily absent: the Senator 
from Arizona (Mr. McCain.)
  The PRESIDING OFFICER. Are there any other Senators in the Chamber 
desiring to vote?
  The result was announced--yeas 88, nays 11, as follows:
