                   MERLE HAGGARD POST OFFICE BUILDING

  The bill (H.R. 1988) to designate the facility of the United States 
Postal Service located at 1730 18th Street in Bakersfield, California, 
as the ``Merle Haggard Post Office Building,'' was ordered to a third 
reading and was read the third time.
