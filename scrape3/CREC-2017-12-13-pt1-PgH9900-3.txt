                     PRIVATE BILLS AND RESOLUTIONS

  Under clause 3 of rule XII,

       Ms. BROWNLEY of California introduced a bill (H.R. 4641) to 
     authorize the President to award the Medal of Honor to John 
     L. Canley for acts of valor during the Vietnam War while a 
     member of the Marine Corps; which was referred to the 
     Committee on Armed Services.
