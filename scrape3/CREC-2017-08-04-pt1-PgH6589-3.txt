                    BILL PRESENTED TO THE PRESIDENT

she presented to the President of the United States, for his approval, 
the following bill:

       H.R. 3364. To provide congressional review and to counter 
     aggression by the Governments of Iran, the Russian 
     Federation, and North Korea, and for other purposes.
