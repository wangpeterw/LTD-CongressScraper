                    BILL PRESENTED TO THE PRESIDENT

  Karen L. Haas, Clerk of the House, reported that on September 28, 
2017, she presented to the President of the United States, for his 
approval, the following bill:

       H.R. 3819. To amend title 38, United States Code, to extend 
     certain expiring provisions of law administered by the 
     Secretary of Veterans Affairs, and for other purposes.
