                               MEMORIALS

  Under clause 3 of rule XII,

       173. The SPEAKER presented a memorial of the House of 
     Representatives of the State of Georgia, relative to House 
     Resolution 1089, urging the United States Congress to pass 
     the federal ``Marketplace Fairness Act'' in support of equity 
     and sales tax fairness; which was referred to the Committee 
     on the Judiciary.
