                      RESOLUTIONS SUBMITTED TODAY

  Mr. McCONNELL. Mr. President, I ask unanimous consent that the Senate 
now proceed to the en bloc consideration of the following Senate 
resolutions which were submitted earlier today: S. Res. 332, S. Res. 
333, and S. Res. 334.
  There being no objection, the Senate proceeded to consider the 
resolutions en bloc.
  Mr. McCONNELL. Mr. President, I ask unanimous consent that the 
resolutions be agreed to, the preamble, where applicable, be agreed to, 
and the motions to reconsider be considered made and laid upon the 
table, all en bloc.
  The PRESIDING OFFICER. Is there objection?
  Without objection, it is so ordered.
  The resolution (S. Res. 332) was agreed to.
  The preamble was agreed to.
  (The resolution, with its preamble, is printed in today's Record 
under ``Submitted Resolutions.'')
  The resolutions (S. Res. 333 and S. Res. 334) were agreed to.
  (The resolutions are printed in today's Record under ``Submitted 
Resolutions.'')
