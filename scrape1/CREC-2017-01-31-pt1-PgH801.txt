   PROVIDING FOR CONSIDERATION OF H.J. RES. 38, DISAPPROVING A RULE 
              SUBMITTED BY THE DEPARTMENT OF THE INTERIOR

ordering the previous question on the resolution (H. Res. 70) providing 
for consideration of the joint resolution (H.J. Res. 38) disapproving 
the rule submitted by the Department of the Interior known as the 
Stream Protection Rule, on which the yeas and nays were ordered.
  The Clerk read the title of the resolution.
  The SPEAKER pro tempore. The question is on ordering the previous 
question.
