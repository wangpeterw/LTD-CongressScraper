                   ORDERS FOR THURSDAY, JULY 27, 2017

  Mr. ENZI. Madam President, I ask unanimous consent that when the 
Senate completes its business today, it adjourn until 10 a.m. on 
Thursday, July 27; further, that following the prayer and pledge, the 
morning hour be deemed expired, the Journal of proceedings be approved 
to date, the time for the two leaders be reserved for their use later 
in the day, and morning business be closed; further, that following 
leader remarks, the Senate resume consideration of H.R. 1628, with the 
time until 2:15 p.m. equally divided between the two leaders or their 
designees; and finally, that at 2:15 p.m., the Senate vote in relation 
to the Daines amendment No. 340, as modified.
  The PRESIDING OFFICER. Without objection, it is so ordered.
