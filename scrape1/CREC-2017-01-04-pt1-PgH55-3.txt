                          MORNING-HOUR DEBATE

  The SPEAKER pro tempore. Pursuant to theorder of the House of January 
3, 2017, the Chair will now recognize Members from lists submitted by 
the majority and minorityleaders for morning-hour debate.
  The Chair will alternate recognition between the parties, with each 
party limited to 1 hourand each Member other than the majority and 
minority leaders and the minority whip limited to 5 minutes, but in no 
event shalldebate continue beyond 11:50 a.m.
