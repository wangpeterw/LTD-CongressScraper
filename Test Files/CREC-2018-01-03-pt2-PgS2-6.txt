                MEASURE PLACED ON THE CALENDAR--S. 2274

  Mr. McCONNELL. Mr. President, I understand that there is a bill at 
the desk due for a second reading.
  The ACTING PRESIDENT pro tempore. The Senator is correct.
  The clerk will read the bill by title for the second time.
  The senior assistant legislative clerk read as follows:

       A bill (S. 2274) to provide for the compensation of Federal 
     employees affected by lapses in appropriations.

  Mr. McCONNELL. Mr. President, in order to place the bill on the 
calendar under the provisions of rule XIV, I object to further 
proceedings.
  The ACTING PRESIDENT pro tempore. Objection having been heard, the 
bill will be placed on the calendar.
