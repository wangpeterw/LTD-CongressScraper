                            PETITIONS, ETC.

  Under clause 3 of rule XII, petitions and papers were laid on the 
clerk's desk and referred as follows:

       65. The SPEAKER presented a petition of Mr. Gregory D. 
     Watson, a citizen of Austin, Texas, relative to requesting 
     that Congress propose for ratification, by special 
     conventions to be held within the several states, pursuant to 
     Article V, an amendment to the United States Constitution 
     which would establish a Bill of Rights for victims of crime; 
     to the Committee on the Judiciary.
