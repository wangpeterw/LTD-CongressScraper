                      PUBLIC BILLS AND RESOLUTIONS

  Under clause 2 of rule XII, public bills and resolutions of the 
following titles were introduced and severally referred, as follows:

           By Mr. LEWIS of Minnesota (for himself, Ms. Brownley of 
             California, Mr. Rokita, and Mr. Garamendi):
       H.R. 2930. A bill to develop a civil unmanned aircraft 
     policy framework, a pilot program, and for other purposes; to 
     the Committee on Transportation and Infrastructure.
           By Mr. CARTWRIGHT:
       H.R. 2931. A bill to expand certain empowerment zone 
     provisions to communities receiving a Worker Adjustment and 
     Retraining Notification Act notice, and for other purposes; 
     to the Committee on Ways and Means, and in addition to the
