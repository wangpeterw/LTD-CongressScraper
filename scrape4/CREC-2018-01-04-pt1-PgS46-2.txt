            SUBMISSION OF CONCURRENT AND SENATE RESOLUTIONS

  The following concurrent resolutions and Senate resolutions were 
read, and referred (or acted upon), as indicated:

           By Mr. CRUZ (for himself, Mrs. Fischer, Mr. Leahy, Mr. 
             Cotton, Mrs. Ernst, Mr. Isakson, Mr. Inhofe, and Mr. 
             Perdue):
       S. Res. 367. A resolution condemning the Government of Iran 
     for its violence against demonstrators and calling for 
     peaceful resolution to the concerns of the citizens of Iran; 
     to the Committee on Foreign Relations.
           By Mr. CORKER (for himself, Mr. Cardin, Mr. Rubio, Mr. 
             Menendez, and Mr. Johnson):
       S. Res. 368. A resolution supporting the right of all 
     Iranian citizens to have their voices heard; to the Committee 
     on Foreign Relations.
