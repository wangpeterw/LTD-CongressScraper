      PRIVILEGED REPORT ON RESOLUTION OF INQUIRY TO THE PRESIDENT

  Mr. GOODLATTE, from the Committee on the Judiciary, submitted an 
adverse privileged report (Rept. No. 115-83) on the resolution (H. Res. 
203) of inquiry requesting the President, and directing the Attorney 
General, to transmit, respectively, certain documents to the House of 
Representatives relating to certain communications by the President of 
the United States, which was referred to the House Calendar and ordered 
to be printed.
