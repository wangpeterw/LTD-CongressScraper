                  MEASURE READ THE FIRST TIME--S. 1757

  Ms. MURKOWSKI. Mr. President, I understand there is a bill at the 
desk, and I ask for its first reading.
  The PRESIDING OFFICER. The clerk will read the bill by title for the 
first time.
  The senior assistant legislative clerk read as follows:
  A bill (S. 1757) to strengthen border security, increase resources 
for enforcement of immigration laws, and for other purposes.
  Ms. MURKOWSKI. Mr. President, I now ask for a second reading and, in 
order to place the bill on the calendar under the provisions of rule 
XIV, I object to my own request.
  The PRESIDING OFFICER. Objection is heard.
  The bill will be read for the second time on the next legislative 
day.
