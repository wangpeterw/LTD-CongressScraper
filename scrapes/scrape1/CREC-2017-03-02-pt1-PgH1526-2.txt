                    BILL PRESENTED TO THE PRESIDENT

  Karen L. Haas, Clerk of the House, reported that on March 01, 2017, 
she presented to the President of the United States, for his approval, 
the following bill:

       H.R. 609. To designate the Department of Veterans Affairs 
     health care center in Center Township, Butler County, 
     Pennsylvania, as the ``Abie Abraham VA Clinic.''
