  STRENGTHENING LOCAL TRANSPORTATION SECURITY CAPABILITIES ACT OF 2018

  The SPEAKER pro tempore. The unfinished business is the vote on the 
motion to suspend the rules and pass the bill (H.R. 5089) to improve 
threat information sharing, integrated operations, and law enforcement 
training for transportation security, and for other purposes, as 
amended, on which the yeas and nays were ordered.
  The Clerk read the title of the bill.
  The SPEAKER pro tempore. The question is on the motion offered by the 
gentleman from Nebraska (Mr. Bacon) that the House suspend the rules 
and pass the bill, as amended.
  This is a 5-minute vote.
  The vote was taken by electronic device, and there were--yeas 397, 
nays 1, not voting 31, as follows:
