                SENATE ENROLLED JOINT RESOLUTION SIGNED

  The Speaker announced his signature to an enrolled Joint Resolution 
of the Senate of the following title:

       S.J. Res. 1. Joint resolution approving the location of a 
     memorial to commemorate and honor the members of the Armed 
     Forces who served on active duty in support of Operation 
     Desert Storm or Operation Desert Shield.
