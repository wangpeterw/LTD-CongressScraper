                    AUTHORITY FOR COMMITTEE TO MEET

  Mr. McCONNELL. Mr. President, I have one request for a committee to 
meet during today's session of the Senate. It has the approval of the 
Majority and Minority leaders.

Senate, the following committee is authorized to meet during today's 
session of the Senate:


                          Committee on Finance

       The Committee on Finance is authorized to meet during the 
     session of the Senate on Monday, February 6, 2017, in S-216, 
     the President's Room of the U.S. Capitol, during a vote on 
     the Senate floor, currently expected to occur circa 6 p.m.
