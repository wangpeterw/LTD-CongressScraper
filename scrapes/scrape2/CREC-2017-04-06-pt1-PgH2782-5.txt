  ADJOURNMENT FROM THURSDAY, APRIL 6, 2017, TO SATURDAY, APRIL 8, 2017

  Mr. SESSIONS. Mr. Speaker, I ask unanimous consent when the House 
adjourns today, it shall adjourn to meet at 11 a.m. on Saturday, April 
8, 2017, unless it sooner has received a message from the Senate 
transmitting its concurrence in House Concurrent Resolution 48, in 
which case the House shall stand adjourned pursuant to that concurrent 
resolution.
  The SPEAKER pro tempore. Is there objection to the request of the 
gentleman from Texas.
  There was no objection.
