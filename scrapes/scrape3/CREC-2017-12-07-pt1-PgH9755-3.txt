                    BILL PRESENTED TO THE PRESIDENT

  Karen L. Haas, Clerk of the House, reported that on December 06, 
2017, she presented to the President of the United States, for his 
approval, the following bill:

       H.R. 228. To amend the Indian Employment, Training and 
     Related Services Demonstration Act of 1992 to facilitate the 
     ability of Indian tribes to integrate the employment, 
     training, and related services from diverse Federal sources, 
     and for other purposes.
