                COMMUNICATION FROM THE DEMOCRATIC LEADER

  The SPEAKER pro tempore laid before the House the following 
communication from the Honorable Nancy Pelosi, Democratic Leader:

                                            February 23, 2018.

     Hon. Paul D. Ryan,
     Speaker of the House of Representatives,
     Washington, DC.
       Dear Speaker Ryan: Pursuant to section 30442(b)(3) of the 
     Bipartisan Budget Act of 2018 (Pub. L. 115-123), I am pleased 
     to appoint the following Members to serve on the Joint Select 
     Committee on Budget and Appropriations Process Reform:
       The Honorable Nita Lowey of New York
       The Honorable John Yarmuth of Kentucky
       The Honorable Lucille Roybal-Allard of California
       The Honorable Derek Kilmer of Washington
       Thank you for your attention to these appointments.
           Sincerely,
                                                     Nancy Pelosi,
     Democratic Leader.
