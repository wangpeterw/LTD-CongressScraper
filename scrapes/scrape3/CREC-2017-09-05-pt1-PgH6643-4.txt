 FINANCIAL STABILITY OVERSIGHT COUNCIL INSURANCE MEMBER CONTINUITY ACT

  The SPEAKER pro tempore. The unfinished business is the vote on the 
motion to suspend the rules and pass the bill (H.R. 3110) to amend the 
Financial Stability Act of 2010 to modify the term of the independent 
member of the Financial Stability Oversight Council, on which the yeas 
and nays were ordered.
  The Clerk read the title of the bill.
  The SPEAKER pro tempore. The question is on the motion offered by the 
gentleman from Illinois (Mr. Hultgren) that the House suspend the rules 
and pass the bill.
  The vote was taken by electronic device, and there were--yeas 407, 
nays 1, not voting 25, as follows:
