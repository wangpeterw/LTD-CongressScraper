  Mrs. NAPOLITANO. Mr. Speaker, I was absent during rollcall votes No. 
341, No. 342, No. 343 and No. 344 due to my spouse's health situation 
in California. Had I been present, I would have voted ``yea'' on the 
Democratic Motion to Recommit H.R. 3003. I would have also voted 
``nay'' on the Final Passage of H.R. 3003--No Sanctuary for Criminals 
Act. I would have also voted ``yea'' on the Democratic Motion to 
Recommit H.R. 3004. I would have also voted ``nay'' on H.R. 3004--
Kate's Law.
