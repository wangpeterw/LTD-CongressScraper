  REPORT ON RESOLUTION PROVIDING FOR CONSIDERATION OF H.R. 2213, ANTI-
             BORDER CORRUPTION REAUTHORIZATION ACT OF 2017

  Ms. CHENEY, from the Committee on Rules, submitted a privileged 
report (Rept. No. 115-162) on the resolution (H. Res. 374) providing 
for consideration of the bill (H.R. 2213) to amend the Anti-Border 
Corruption Act of 2010 to authorize certain polygraph waiver authority, 
and for other purposes, which was referred to the House Calendar and 
ordered to be printed.
