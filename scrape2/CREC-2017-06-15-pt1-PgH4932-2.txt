HOUR OF MEETING ON TOMORROW AND ADJOURNMENT FROM FRIDAY, JUNE 16, 2017, 
                       TO TUESDAY, JUNE 20, 2017

  Mr. McCARTHY. Mr. Speaker, I ask unanimous consent that when the 
House adjourns today, it adjourn to meet at 9:30 a.m. tomorrow; and 
further, when the House adjourns on that day, it adjourn to meet on 
Tuesday, June 20, 2017, when it shall convene at noon for morning-hour 
debate and 2 p.m. for legislative business.
  The SPEAKER pro tempore. Is there objection to the request of the 
gentleman from California?
  There was no objection.
