 MOMENT OF SILENCE IN REMEMBRANCE OF MEMBERS OF ARMED FORCES AND THEIR 
                                FAMILIES

  The SPEAKER. The Chair asks that the House now observe a moment of 
silence in honor of those who have been killed or wounded in service to 
our country and all those who serve and their families.
