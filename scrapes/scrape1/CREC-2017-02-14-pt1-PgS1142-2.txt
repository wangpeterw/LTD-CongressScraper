  PROVIDING FOR CONGRESSIONAL DISAPPROVAL OF A RULE SUBMITTED BY THE 
           SOCIAL SECURITY ADMINISTRATION--MOTION TO PROCEED

  Mr. McCONNELL. Mr. President, I move to proceed to H.J. Res. 40.
  The PRESIDING OFFICER. The clerk will report the motion.
  The legislative clerk read as follows:

       Motion to proceed to H.J. Res. 40, a joint resolution 
     providing for congressional disapproval under chapter 8 of 
     title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the 
     Social Security Administration relating to Implementation of 
     the NICS Improvement Amendments Act of 2007.

  The PRESIDING OFFICER. The question is on agreeing to the motion.
  The motion was agreed to.
