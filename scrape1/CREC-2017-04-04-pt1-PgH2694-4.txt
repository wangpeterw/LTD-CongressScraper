                     EXECUTIVE COMMUNICATIONS, ETC.

  973. Under clause 2 of rule XIV, a letter from the Assistant Legal 
Adviser, Office of Treaty Affairs, Department of State, transmitting a 
report concerning international agreements other than treaties entered 
into by the United States to be transmitted to the Congress within the 
sixty-day period specified in the Case-Zablocki Act, pursuant to 1 
U.S.C. 112b(a); Public Law 92-403, Sec. 1(a) (as amended by Public Law 
108-458, Sec. 7121(b)); (118 Stat. 3807), was taken from the Speaker's 
table, referred to the Committee on Foreign Affairs.
