                   DR. JOHN F. NASH, JR. POST OFFICE

  The bill (H.R. 2302) to designate the facility of the United States 
Postal Service located at 259 Nassau Street, Suite 2 in Princeton, New 
Jersey, as the ``Dr. John F. Nash, Jr. Post Office,'' was ordered to a 
third reading and was read the third time.
