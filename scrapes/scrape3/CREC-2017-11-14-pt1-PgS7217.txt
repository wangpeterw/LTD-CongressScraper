                   AMENDMENTS SUBMITTED AND PROPOSED

       SA 1584. Mr. McCONNELL (for Mrs. Feinstein (for herself, 
     Mr. Thune, Mr. Nelson, Ms. Collins, Mr. Donnelly, Mr. Young, 
     Mr. Durbin, and Ms. Warren)) proposed an amendment to the 
     bill S. 534, to prevent the sexual abuse of minors and 
     amateur athletes by requiring the prompt reporting of sexual 
     abuse to law enforcement authorities, and for other purposes.
       SA 1585. Mr. McCONNELL (for Mr. Wyden) proposed an 
     amendment to the resolution S. Res. 318, honoring the 
     Portland Thorns FC as the champion of the National Women's 
     Soccer League in 2017.
