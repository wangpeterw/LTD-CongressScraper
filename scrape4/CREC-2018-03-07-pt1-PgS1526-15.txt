                    HOWARD B. PATE, JR. POST OFFICE

  The bill (H.R. 3369) to designate the facility of the United States 
Postal Service located at 225 North Main Street in Spring Lake, North 
Carolina, as the ``Howard B. Pate, Jr. Post Office,'' was ordered to a 
third reading and was read the third time.
