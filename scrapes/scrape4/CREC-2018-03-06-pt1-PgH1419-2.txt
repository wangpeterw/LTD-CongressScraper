                  COMPREHENSIVE REGULATORY REVIEW ACT

  The SPEAKER pro tempore. The unfinished business is the vote on the 
motion to recommit on the bill (H.R. 4607) to amend the Economic Growth 
and Regulatory Paperwork Reduction Act of 1996 to ensure that Federal 
financial regulators perform a comprehensive review of regulations to 
identify outdated or otherwise unnecessary regulatory requirements 
imposed on covered persons, and for other purposes, offered by the 
gentlewoman from Massachusetts (Ms. Clark), on which the yeas and nays 
were ordered.
  The Clerk will redesignate the motion.
  The Clerk redesignated the motion.
  The SPEAKER pro tempore. The question is on the motion to recommit.
