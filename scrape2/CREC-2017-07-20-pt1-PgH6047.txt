                ANNOUNCEMENT BY THE SPEAKER PRO TEMPORE

  The SPEAKER pro tempore (Mr. Poe of Texas). Pursuant to clause 8 of 
rule XX, the Chair will postpone further proceedings today on the 
motion to suspend the rules on which a recorded vote or the yeas and 
nays are ordered, or on which the vote incurs objection under clause 6 
of rule XX.
  The House will resume proceedings on the postponed question at a 
later time.
