                    AMENDMENTS SUBMITTED AND PROPOSED

       SA 2044. Mr. CORKER submitted an amendment intended to be 
     proposed by him to the bill H.R. 390, to provide emergency 
     relief for victims of genocide, crimes against humanity, and 
     war crimes in Iraq and Syria, for accountability for 
     perpetrators of these crimes, and for other purposes; which 
     was ordered to lie on the table.
