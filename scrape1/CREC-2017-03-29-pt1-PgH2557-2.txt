              JOINT RESOLUTIONS PRESENTED TO THE PRESIDENT

  Karen L. Haas, Clerk of the House, reported that on March 27, 2017, 
she presented to the President of the United States, for his approval, 
the following joint resolutions:

       H.J. Res. 83. Disapproving the rule submitted by the 
     Department of Labor relating to ``Clarification of Employer's 
     Continuing Obligation to Make and Maintain an Accurate Record 
     of Each Recordable Injury and Illness''.
       H.J. Res. 69. Providing for congressional disapproval under 
     chapter 8 of title 5, United States Code, of the final rule 
     of the Department of the Interior relating to ``Non-
     Subsistence Take of Wildlife, and Public Participation and 
     Closure Procedures, on National Wildlife Refuges in Alaska''.
