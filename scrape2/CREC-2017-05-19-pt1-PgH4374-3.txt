                COMMUNICATION FROM THE DEMOCRATIC LEADER

  The SPEAKER pro tempore laid before the House the following 
communication from the Honorable Nancy Pelosi, Democratic Leader:

                                                     May 18, 2017.
     Hon. Paul Ryan,
     Speaker of the House, U.S. Capitol,
     Washington, DC.
       Dear Speaker Ryan: Pursuant to Section 114(b) of the John 
     C. Stennis Center for Public Service Training and Development 
     Act (2 U.S.C. 1103), I hereby reappoint the Honorable Terri 
     A. Sewell of Alabama to the Board of Trustees for the John C. 
     Stennis Center

[[Page H4375]]

     for Public Service Training and Development for a term of six 
     years.
           Sincerely,
                                                     Nancy Pelosi,
     Democratic Leader.
