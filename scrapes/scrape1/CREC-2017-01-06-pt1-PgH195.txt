                               MEMORIALS

  Under clause 3 of rule XII,

       1. The SPEAKER presented a memorial of the Senate of the 
     State of California, relative to Senate Resolution No. 7, 
     condemning in the strongest terms bigoted, racist, or 
     misinformed descriptions of the immigrant community that 
     serve only to foment hatred and violence and that the Senate 
     supports a comprehensive and workable approach to solving our 
     nation's historically broken immigration system; which was 
     referred to the Committee on the Judiciary.
