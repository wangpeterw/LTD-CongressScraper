SENATE RESOLUTION 101--PROVIDING FOR MEMBERS ON THE PART OF THE SENATE 
OF THE JOINT COMMITTEE ON PRINTING AND THE JOINT COMMITTEE OF CONGRESS 
                             ON THE LIBRARY

  Mr. SHELBY (for himself and Ms. Klobuchar) submitted the following 
resolution; which was considered and agreed to:

                              S. Res. 101

       Resolved, That the following named Members be, and they are 
     hereby, elected members of the following joint committees of 
     Congress:
       Joint Committee on Printing: Mr. Shelby, Mr. Roberts, Mr. 
     Wicker, Ms. Klobuchar, and Mr. Udall.
       Joint Committee of Congress on the Library: Mr. Shelby, Mr. 
     Roberts, Mr. Blunt, Ms. Klobuchar, and Mr. Leahy.
