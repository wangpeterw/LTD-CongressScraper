  The following concurrent resolutions and Senate resolutions were

           By Mr. ALEXANDER (for himself, Mr. Bennet, Mr. Booker, 
             Mr. Burr, Mr. Carper, Mr. Cassidy, Mr. Coons, Mr. 
             Cornyn, Mr. Cruz, Mrs. Feinstein, Mr. Gardner, Mr.

             McCain, Mr. Rubio, Mr. Scott, Mr. Tillis, Mr. Toomey, 
             Mr. Wicker, Mr. Young, Mr. Boozman, and Mr. Hatch):
       S. Res. 148. A resolution congratulating the students, 
     parents, teachers, and leaders of charter schools across the 
     United States for making ongoing contributions to education, 
     and supporting the ideals and goals of the 18th annual 
     National Charter Schools Week, to be held May 1 through May 
     5, 2017; considered and agreed to.
