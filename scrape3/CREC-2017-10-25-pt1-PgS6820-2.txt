                              APPOINTMENTS

  The PRESIDING OFFICER. On Tuesday, October 24, 2017, the Chair, on 
behalf of the President pro tempore, and upon the recommendation of the 
majority leader, pursuant to Public Law 96-388, as amended by Public 
Law 97-84, and Public Law 106-292, reappointed and appointed the 
following Senators to the United States Holocaust Memorial Council: the 
Honorable Orrin G. Hatch of Utah, The Honorable Marco Rubio of Florida, 
and The Honorable Tim Scott of South Carolina.
