                NOTICE OF INTENT TO OBJECT TO PROCEEDING

  I, Senator Charles E. Grassley, intend to object to proceeding to the 
nomination of David J. Ryder, of New Jersey, to be Director of the 
Mint, and Isabel Marie Keenan Patelunas, of Pennsylvania, to be 
Assistant Secretary of Intelligence and Analysis, Department of the 
Treasury, dated December 20, 2017.
