                            PETITIONS, ETC.

  Under clause 3 of rule XII,

       69. The SPEAKER presented a petition of Mr. Gregory D. 
     Watson, a citizen of Austin, Texas, relative to urging 
     Congress to enact legislation that would prohibit the use of 
     any Federal Government funds to settle out of court any 
     allegation that any current or former elected or appointed 
     official within the Federal Government, or any current or 
     former Federal employee, committed sexual harassment while 
     serving in the executive, judicial, or legislative branches 
     of the Federal Government; which was referred jointly to the 
     Committees on House Administration, the Judiciary, and 
     Oversight and Government Reform.
