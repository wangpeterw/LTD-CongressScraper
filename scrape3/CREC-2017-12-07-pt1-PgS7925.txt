                    APPOINTMENT OF CONFEREES--H.R. 1

  The PRESIDING OFFICER. Under the authority granted on a motion 
yesterday, the Chair appoints conferees on the part of the Senate on 
the disagreeing votes of the two Houses.
  The Presiding Officer appointed Mr. Hatch, Mr. Enzi, Ms. Murkowski, 
Mr. Cornyn, Mr. Thune, Mr. Portman, Mr. Scott, Mr. Toomey, Mr. Wyden, 
Mr. Sanders, Mrs. Murray, Ms. Cantwell, Ms. Stabenow, Mr. Menendez, and 
Mr. Carper conferees on the part of the Senate.
  The PRESIDING OFFICER. The majority leader.
