                           ORDER OF PROCEDURE

  Mr. McCONNELL. Mr. President, I ask unanimous consent that the debate 
time on the nomination of Judge Gorsuch during Wednesday's session of 
the Senate be divided as follows: that following leader remarks the 
time until 11 a.m. be equally divided; that the time from 11 a.m. until 
12 noon be under the control of the majority; that the time from 12 
noon until 1 p.m. be

[[Page S2221]]

under the control of the minority; further, that the debate time until 
9 p.m. on Wednesday be divided in 1-hour alternating blocks.
  The PRESIDING OFFICER. Is there objection?
  Without objection, it is so ordered.
