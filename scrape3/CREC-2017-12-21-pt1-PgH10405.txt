         SEXUAL ASSAULT FORENSIC EVIDENCE REPORTING ACT OF 2017

  The SPEAKER pro tempore (Mr. Marshall). The unfinished business is 
the question on suspending the rules and passing the bill (S. 1766) to 
reauthorize the SAFER Act of 2013, and for other purposes.
  The Clerk read the title of the bill.
  The SPEAKER pro tempore. The question is on the motion offered by the 
gentlewoman from Georgia (Mrs. Handel) that the House suspend the rules 
and pass the bill.
  The question was taken; and (two-thirds being in the affirmative) the 
rules were suspended and the bill was passed.
  A motion to reconsider was laid on the table.
