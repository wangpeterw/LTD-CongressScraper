                            PETITIONS, ETC.

  Under clause 3 of rule XII,

       89. The SPEAKER presented a petition of city Council of 
     Akron, OH, relative to Resolution No. 64-2018, calling on the 
     Ohio General Assembly to enact a ban on the sale of assault-
     style weapons, ammunition magazines in excess of ten rounds, 
     and modifications that simulate automated fire such as bump 
     stocks and trigger cranks; and declaring an emergency; which 
     was referred to the Committee on the Judiciary.
