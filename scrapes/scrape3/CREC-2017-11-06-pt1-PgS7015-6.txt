                   EXECUTIVE AND OTHER COMMUNICATIONS

  The following communications were laid before the Senate, together 
with accompanying papers, reports, and documents, and were referred as 
indicated:

        EC-3380. A communication from the General Counsel of the 
     National Credit Union Administration, transmitting, pursuant 
     to law, the report of a rule entitled ``Revisions to the 
     Freedom of Information Act Regulation'' (RIN3133-AD44) 
     received in the Office of the President of the Senate on 
     November 1, 2017; to the Committee on Banking, Housing, and 
     Urban Affairs.
        EC-3381. A communication from the Acting Assistant 
     Secretary for Legislation, Department of Health and Human 
     Services, transmitting, pursuant to law, a report relative to 
     the Family Violence Prevention and Services Program for 
     fiscal years 2013-2014; to the Committee on Health, 
     Education, Labor, and Pensions.
        EC-3382. A communication from the Acting Assistant 
     Secretary for Legislation, Department of Health and Human 
     Services, transmitting, pursuant to law, a report entitled 
     ``2016 Annual Report to the Congress on the Native Hawaiian 
     Revolving Loan Fund''; to the Committee on Indian Affairs.
