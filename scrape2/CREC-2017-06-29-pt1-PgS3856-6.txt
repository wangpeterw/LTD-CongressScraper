                           MEASURES REFERRED

  The following bills were read the first and the second times by 
unanimous consent, and referred as indicated:

       H.R. 1215. An act to improve patient access to health care 
     services and provide improved medical care by reducing the 
     excessive burden the liability system places on the health 
     care delivery system; to the Committee on the Judiciary.
       H.R. 1500. An act to redesignate the small triangular 
     property located in Washington, DC, and designated by the 
     National Park Service as reservation 302 as ``Robert Emmet 
     Park'', and for other purposes; to the Committee on Energy 
     and Natural Resources.
