                               MEMORIALS

  Under clause 3 of rule XII, memorials were presented and referred as 
follows:

       2. The SPEAKERpresented a memorial of the Senate of the 
     Commonwealth of Puerto Rico, relative to Senate Resolution 
     1449, to urge the Presidentof the United States, Barack 
     Obama, to grant a Presidential pardon to Oscar Lopez-Rivera; 
     to the Committee on the Judiciary.
       3. Also, a memorial of the Senate of the Commonwealth of 
     Massachusetts, relative to a Resolution,memorializing 
     Congress to consider the Bridge Act and protect eligible 
     young undocumented immigrants who reside in the UnitedStates; 
     to the Committee on the Judiciary.
