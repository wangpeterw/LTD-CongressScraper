REPORT ON RESOLUTION PROVIDING FOR CONSIDERATION OF H.R. 428, RED RIVER 
 GRADIENT BOUNDARY SURVEY ACT, AND PROVIDING FOR CONSIDERATION OF H.J. 
RES. 42, DISAPPROVING RULE SUBMITTED BY DEPARTMENT OF LABOR RELATING TO 
          DRUG TESTING OF UNEMPLOYMENT COMPENSATION APPLICANTS

  Mr. COLE, from the Committee on Rules, submitted a privileged report 
(Rept. No. 115-10) on the resolution (H. Res. 99) providing for 
consideration of the bill (H.R. 428) to survey the gradient boundary 
along the Red River in the States of Oklahoma and Texas, and for other 
purposes, and providing for consideration of the joint resolution (H.J. 
Res. 42) disapproving the rule submitted by the Department of Labor 
relating to drug testing of unemployment compensation applicants, which 
was referred to the House Calendar and ordered to be printed.
