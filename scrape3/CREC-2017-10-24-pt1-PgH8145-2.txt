                            PETITIONS, ETC.

  Under clause 3 of rule XII,

       64. The SPEAKER presented a petition of Mr. Gregory D. 
     Watson, a citizen of Austin, Texas, relative to remonstrating 
     against the enactment of any legislation by Congress that 
     would end the status of ``Columbus Day'' as an official 
     Federal holiday; which was referred to the Committee on 
     Oversight and Government Reform.
