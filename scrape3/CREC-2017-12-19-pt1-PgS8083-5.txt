                            MORNING BUSINESS

  Mr. INHOFE. Mr. President, I ask unanimous consent that the Senate be

therein, equally divided, until 3 p.m.

  Mr. INHOFE. I also ask unanimous consent that at the conclusion of 
the remarks of the Senator from Virginia, I be recognized for such time 
as I may consume.
  The PRESIDING OFFICER. Without objection, it is so ordered.
