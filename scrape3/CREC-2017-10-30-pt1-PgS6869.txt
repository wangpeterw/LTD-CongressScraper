                            VOTE EXPLANATION

 Mr. MENENDEZ. Mr. President, I was unavailable for rollcall 
vote No. 253, on the nomination of Trevor McFadden to be U.S. District 
Judge for the District of Columbia. Had I been present, I would have 
voted yea.
  Mr. President, I was unavailable for rollcall vote No. 254, on the 
motion to invoke cloture on the nomination of Amy Barrett to be U.S. 
Circuit Judge for the Seventh Circuit. Had I been present, I would have 
voted nay.
