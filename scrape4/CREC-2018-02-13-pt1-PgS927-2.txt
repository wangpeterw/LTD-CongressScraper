                ORDERS FOR WEDNESDAY, FEBRUARY 14, 2018

  Mr. McCONNELL. Mr. President, I ask unanimous consent that when the 
Senate completes its business today, it adjourn until 10 a.m., 
Wednesday, February 14; that following the prayer and pledge, the 
morning hour be deemed expired, the Journal of proceedings be approved 
to date, the time for the two leaders be reserved for their use later 
in the day, and morning business be closed; I further ask that 
following leader remarks, the Senate resume and vote on the motion to 
proceed to H.R. 2579.
  The PRESIDING OFFICER. Without objection, it is so ordered.
