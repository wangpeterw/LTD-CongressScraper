     JOINT SESSION OF THE TWO HOUSES--COUNTING OF ELECTORAL BALLOTS


                                 Recess

  The ACTING PRESIDENT pro tempore. Under the previous order, the 
Senate stands in recess to proceed as a body to the Hall of the House 
of Representatives under the provisions of S. Con. Res. 2 for the 
counting of the electoral ballots.
  Thereupon, the Senate, at 12:48 p.m., took a recess, and the Senate, 
preceded by the Secretary of the Senate, Julie E. Adams, and the Deputy 
Sergeant at Arms, James Morhard, proceeded to the Hall of the House of 
Representatives for the purpose of counting electoral ballots.
