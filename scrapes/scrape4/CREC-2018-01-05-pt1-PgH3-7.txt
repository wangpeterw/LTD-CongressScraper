   COMMUNICATION FROM THE HONORABLE MATTHEW A. CARTWRIGHT, MEMBER OF 
                                CONGRESS

  The SPEAKER pro tempore laid before the House the following 
communication from the Honorable Matthew A. Cartwright, Member of 
Congress:

                                     House of Representatives,

                                Washington, DC, December 27, 2017.
     Hon. Paul D. Ryan,
     Speaker, House of Representatives,
     Washington, DC.
       Dear Mr. Speaker: This is to notify you formally pursuant 
     to Rule VIII of the Rules of the House of Representatives 
     that I have been served with a subpoena for testimony, issued 
     by the Court of Common Pleas for Lackawanna County, 
     Pennsylvania.
       After consulting with the Office of General Counsel, I will 
     make the determinations required by Rule VIII.
           Sincerely,
