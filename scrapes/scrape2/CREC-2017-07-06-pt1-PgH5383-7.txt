                COMMUNICATION FROM THE DEMOCRATIC LEADER

  The SPEAKER pro tempore laid before the House the following 
communication from the Honorable Nancy Pelosi, Democratic Leader:

                                                    June 30, 2017.
     Hon. Paul Ryan,
     Speaker of the House,
     Washington, DC.
       Dear Speaker Ryan: Pursuant to 2 U.S.C. 2081, I am pleased 
     to reappoint the Honorable Marcy Kaptur of Ohio to the United 
     States Capitol Preservation Commission.
       Thank you for your consideration of this appointment.
           Sincerely,
                                                     Nancy Pelosi,
     Democratic Leader.
