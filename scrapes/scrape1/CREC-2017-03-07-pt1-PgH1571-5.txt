               FALEOMAVAEGA ENI FA'AUA'A HUNKIN VA CLINIC

  The SPEAKER pro tempore. The unfinished business is the vote on the 
motion to suspend the rules and pass the bill (H.R. 1362) to name the 
Department of Veterans Affairs community-based outpatient clinic in 
Pago Pago, American Samoa, the Faleomavaega Eni Fa'aua'a Hunkin VA 
Clinic, on which the yeas and nays were ordered.
  The Clerk read the title of the bill.
  The SPEAKER pro tempore. The question is on the motion offered by the 
gentlewoman from America Samoa (Mrs. Radewagen) that the House suspend 
the rules and pass the bill.
  The vote was taken by electronic device, and there were--yeas 411, 
nays 2, not voting 16, as follows:
