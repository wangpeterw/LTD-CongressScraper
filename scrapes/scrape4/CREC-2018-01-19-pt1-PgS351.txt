                NOTICE OF INTENT TO OBJECT TO PROCEEDING

  I, Senator Charles E. Grassley, intend to object to proceeding to the 
nomination of David J. Ryder, of New Jersey, to be Director of the Mint 
for the Department of Treasury, dated January 19, 2018.
  Mr. MENENDEZ. I suggest the absence of a quorum.
  The PRESIDING OFFICER (Mr. Alexander). The clerk will call the roll.
  The bill clerk proceeded to call the roll.
  (Mr. TILLIS assumed the Chair.)
  Mr. McCONNELL. Mr. President, I ask unanimous consent that the order 
for the quorum call be rescinded.
  The PRESIDING OFFICER (Mr. Rounds). Without objection, it is so 
ordered.
