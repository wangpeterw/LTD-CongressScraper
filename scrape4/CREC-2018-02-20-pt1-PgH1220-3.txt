                               MEMORIALS

  Under clause 3 of rule XII,

       161. The SPEAKER presented a memorial of the Senate of the 
     State of California, relative to Senate Resolution No. 72, 
     urging the President of the United States and the United 
     States Congress to express their support for a woman's 
     fundamental right to control her own reproductive decisions, 
     as well as their support for access to comprehensive 
     reproductive health care, including the services provided by 
     Planned Parenthood; which was referred to the Committee on 
     Energy and Commerce.
