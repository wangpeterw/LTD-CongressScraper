                   ORDERS FOR TUESDAY, MARCH 14, 2017

  Mr. McCONNELL. Mr. President, I ask unanimous consent that when the 
Senate completes its business today, it adjourn until 2 p.m., Tuesday, 
March 14; further, that following the prayer and pledge, the morning 
hour be deemed expired, the Journal of proceedings be approved to date, 
the time for the two leaders be reserved for their use later in the 
day, and morning business be closed; finally, that following leader 
remarks, the Senate resume consideration of H.J. Res. 42.
  The PRESIDING OFFICER. Is there objection?
  Without objection, it is so ordered.
