            U.S. NAVY SEAMAN DAKOTA KYLE RIGSBY POST OFFICE

  The SPEAKER pro tempore. The unfinished business is the vote on the 
motion to suspend the rules and pass the bill (H.R. 3183) to designate 
the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 13683 James 
Madison Highway in Palmyra, Virginia, as the ``U.S. Navy Seaman Dakota 
Kyle Rigsby Post Office'', on which the yeas and nays were ordered.
  The Clerk read the title of the bill.
  The SPEAKER pro tempore. The question is on the motion offered by the 
gentleman from Alabama (Mr. Palmer) that the House suspend the rules 
and pass the bill.
  The vote was taken by electronic device, and there were--yeas 389, 
nays 0, not voting 41, as follows:
