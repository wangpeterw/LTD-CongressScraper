               UNANIMOUS CONSENT AGREEMENT--H.J. RES. 76

  Mr. PERDUE. Mr. President, I ask unanimous consent that if the Senate 
receives H.J. Res. 76 from the House, and if the text of H.J. Res. 76 
is identical to the text at the desk, that the joint resolution be 
considered passed, the preamble be considered agreed to, and the 
motions to reconsider be considered made and laid upon the table with 
no intervening action or debate.
  The PRESIDING OFFICER. Is there objection?
  Without objection, it is so ordered.
  The PRESIDING OFFICER. The Senator from Delaware.
