             FAIR ACCESS TO INVESTMENT RESEARCH ACT OF 2017

  The SPEAKER pro tempore. The unfinished business is the vote on the 
motion to suspend the rules and pass the

[[Page H2988]]

bill (H.R. 910) to direct the Securities and Exchange Commission to 
provide a safe harbor related to certain investment fund research 
reports, and for other purposes, as amended, on which the yeas and nays 
were ordered.
  The Clerk read the title of the bill.
  The SPEAKER pro tempore. The question is on the motion offered by the 
gentleman from Arkansas (Mr. Hill) that the House suspend the rules and 
pass the bill, as amended.
  The vote was taken by electronic device, and there were--yeas 405, 
nays 2, not voting 23, as follows:
