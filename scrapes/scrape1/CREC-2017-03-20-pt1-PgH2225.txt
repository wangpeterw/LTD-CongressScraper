     REPORT ON RESOLUTION PROVIDING FOR CONSIDERATION OF H.R. 372, 
            COMPETITIVE HEALTH INSURANCE REFORM ACT OF 2017

  Mr. BYRNE, from the Committee on Rules, submitted a privileged report 
(Rept. No. 115-50) on the resolution (H. Res. 209) providing for 
consideration of the bill (H.R. 372) to restore the application of the 
Federal antitrust laws to the business of health insurance to protect 
competition and consumers, which was referred to the House Calendar and 
ordered to be printed.
