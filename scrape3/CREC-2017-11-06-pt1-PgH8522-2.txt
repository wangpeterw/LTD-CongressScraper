                               MEMORIALS

  Under clause 3 of rule XII,

       144. The SPEAKER presented a memorial of the Senate of the 
     State of Michigan, relative to Senate Resolution No. 73, to 
     memorialize the United States Congress to do all it can to 
     support atomic veterans, their spouses, and dependents in 
     receiving medical care and disability compensation; which was 
     referred to the Committee on Veterans' Affairs.
