                    BILLS PRESENTED TO THE PRESIDENT

  Karen L. Haas, Clerk of the House, reported that on September 29, 
2017, she presented to the President of the United States, for his 
approval, the following bills.

       H.R. 3823. To amend title 49, United States Code, to extend 
     authorizations for the airport improvement program, to amend 
     the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to extend the funding and 
     expenditure authority of the Airport and Airway Trust Fund, 
     to provide disaster tax relief, and for other purposes.
       H.R. 2519. To require the Secretary of the Treasury to mint 
     commemorative coins in recognition of the 100th anniversary 
     of The American Legion.
