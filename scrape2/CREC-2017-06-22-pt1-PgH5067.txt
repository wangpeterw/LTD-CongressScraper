  REPORT ON H.R. 2998, MILITARY CONSTRUCTION AND VETERANS AFFAIRS AND 
               RELATED AGENCIES APPROPRIATIONS ACT, 2018

  Mr. DENT, from the Committee on Appropriations, submitted a 
privileged report (Rept. No. 115-188) on the bill (H.R. 2998) making 
appropriations for military construction, the Department of Veterans 
Affairs, and related agencies for the fiscal year ending September 30, 
2018, and for other purposes, which was referred to the Union Calendar 
and ordered to be printed.
  The SPEAKER pro tempore. Pursuant to clause 1, rule XXI, all points 
of order are reserved on the bill.
