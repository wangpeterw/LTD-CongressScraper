                    BILL PRESENTED TO THE PRESIDENT

  Karen L. Haas, Clerk of the House, reported that on March 23, 2017, 
she presented to the President of the United States, for his approval, 
the following bill:

       H.R. 1228. To provide for the appointment of members of the 
     Board of Directors of the Office of Compliance to replace 
     members whose terms expire during 2017, and for other 
     purposes.
