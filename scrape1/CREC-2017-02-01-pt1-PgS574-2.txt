                            VOTE EXPLANATION

  Mr. DURBIN. Mr. President, I was necessarily absent for the votes on 
the motion to proceed to legislative session and the motion to procced 
to a joint resolution disapproving the rule submitted by the Department 
of the Interior known as the Stream Protection Rule, H.J. Res. 38.
  On vote No. 41, had I been present, I would have voted ``nay'' on the 
motion to proceed to legislative session.
  On vote No. 42, had I been present, I would have voted ``nay'' on the 
motion to proceed to H.J. Res. 38.
