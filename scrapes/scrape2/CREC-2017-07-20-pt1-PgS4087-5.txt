                RECESS SUBJECT TO THE CALL OF THE CHAIR

  The ACTING PRESIDENT pro tempore. In my capacity as a Senator from 
the State of Alabama, I ask unanimous consent that the Senate recess 
subject to the call of the Chair.
  There being no objection, the Senate, at 10:03 a.m., recessed subject 
to the call of the Chair and reassembled at 10:11 a.m. when called to 
order by the Acting President pro tempore.
