                ANNOUNCEMENT BY THE SPEAKER PRO TEMPORE

  The SPEAKER pro tempore. Pursuant to clause 8 of rule XX, proceedings 
will resume on questions previously postponed.
  Votes will be taken in the following order:
  The motion to recommit on H.R. 2954;
  Passage of H.R. 2954, if ordered;
  The motion to concur in the Senate amendment to H.R. 195 with an 
amendment;
  The motion to suspend the rules on H.R. 1660; and
  Agreeing to the Speaker's approval of the Journal, if ordered.
  The first electronic vote will be conducted as a 15-minute vote. 
Remaining electronic votes will be conducted as 
5-minute votes.
