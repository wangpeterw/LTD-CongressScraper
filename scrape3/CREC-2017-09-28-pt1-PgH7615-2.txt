      PRIVILEGED REPORT ON RESOLUTION OF INQUIRY TO THE PRESIDENT

  Mr. GOODLATTE, from the Committee on the Judiciary, submitted an 
adverse privileged report (Rept. No. 115-335) on the resolution (H. 
Res. 488) of inquiry requesting the President and directing the 
Attorney General to transmit, respectively, certain documents to the 
House of Representatives relating to the removal of former Federal 
Bureau of Investigation Director James Comey, which was referred to the 
House Calendar and ordered to be printed.
