   PRIVILEGED REPORT ON RESOLUTION OF INQUIRY DIRECTING THE ATTORNEY 
 GENERAL TO TRANSMIT CERTAIN DOCUMENTS TO THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES 
          RELATING TO THE FINANCIAL PRACTICES OF THE PRESIDENT

  Mr. GOODLATTE, from the Committee on the Judiciary, submitted an 
adverse privileged report (Rept. No. 115-28) on the resolution (H. Res. 
111) of inquiry directing the Attorney General to transmit certain 
documents to the House of Representatives relating to the financial 
practices of the President, which was referred to the House Calendar 
and ordered to be printed.
