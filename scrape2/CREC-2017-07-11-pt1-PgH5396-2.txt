                COMMUNICATION FROM THE DEMOCRATIC LEADER

  The SPEAKER pro tempore laid before the House the following 
communication from the Honorable Nancy Pelosi, Democratic Leader:

                                                    July 11, 2017.
     Hon. Paul D. Ryan,
     Speaker of the House of Representatives,
     Washington, DC.
       Dear Speaker Ryan: Pursuant to section 703(c) of the Public 
     Interest Declassification Act of 2000 (50 U.S.C.) 3161 note), 
     I hereby appoint Mr. John F. Tierney of Massachusetts to the 
     Public Interest Declassification Board.
       Thank you for your consideration of this appointment.
           Sincerely,
                                                     Nancy Pelosi,
     Democratic Leader.
