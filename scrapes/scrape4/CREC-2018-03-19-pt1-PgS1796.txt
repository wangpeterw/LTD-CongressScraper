                              APPOINTMENTS

  The PRESIDING OFFICER. The Chair, on behalf of the President pro 
tempore, pursuant to Public Law 96-388, as amended by Public Law 97-84, 
and Public Law 106-292, appoints the following Senator to the United 
States Holocaust Memorial Council: the Honorable Benjamin L. Cardin of 
Maryland.
  The Chair, on behalf of the Vice President, pursuant to 10 U.S.C. 
4355(a), appoints the following Senator to the Board of Visitors of the 
U.S. Military Academy: the Honorable Joe Manchin III of West Virginia, 
from the Committee on Appropriations.
