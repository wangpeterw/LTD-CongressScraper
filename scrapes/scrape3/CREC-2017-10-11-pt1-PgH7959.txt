LIMITING TO A CERTAIN DATE APPLICABILITY OF PROVISIONS OF SECTION 7 OF 
      THE WAR POWERS RESOLUTION TO HOUSE CONCURRENT RESOLUTION 81

  Mr. COLLINS of Georgia. Mr. Speaker, I ask unanimous consent that the 
provisions of section 7 of the War Powers Resolution (50 U.S.C. 1546) 
shall apply to H. Con. Res. 81 not earlier than November 2, 2017, but 
on the same terms that would have adhered on October 13, 2017.
  The SPEAKER pro tempore. Is there objection to the request of the 
gentleman from Georgia?
  There was no objection.
