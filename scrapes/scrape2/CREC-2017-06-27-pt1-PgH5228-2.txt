 REPORT ON RESOLUTION PROVIDING FOR CONSIDERATION OF H.R. 3004, KATE'S 
LAW, AND PROVIDING FOR PROCEEDINGS DURING THE PERIOD FROM JULY 3, 2017, 
                         THROUGH JULY 10, 2017

  Mr. COLLINS of Georgia (during the Special Order of Mr. Murphy of 
Pennsylvania), from the Committee on Rules, submitted a privileged 
report (Rept. No. 115-196) on the resolution (H. Res. 415) providing 
for consideration of the bill (H.R. 3004) to amend section 276 of the 
Immigration and Nationality Act relating to reentry of removed aliens, 
and providing for proceedings during the period from July 3, 2017, 
through July 10, 2017, which was referred to the House Calendar and 
ordered to be printed.
