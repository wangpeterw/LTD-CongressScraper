               COMMUNICATION FROM THE CLERK OF THE HOUSE

  The SPEAKER pro tempore laid before the House the following 
communication from the Clerk of the House of Representatives:

                                              Office of the Clerk,


                                     House of Representatives,

                                    Washington, DC, March 1, 2018.
     Hon. Paul D. Ryan,
     Speaker, House of Representatives,
     Washington, DC.
       Dear Mr. Speaker: Pursuant to the permission granted in 
     Clause 2(h) of Rule II of the Rules of the U.S. House of 
     Representatives, the Clerk received the following message 
     from the Secretary of the Senate on March 1, 2018, at 10:59 
     a.m.:
       That the Senate agreed to S. J. Res. 53.
       That the Senate passed without amendment H.R. 535.
       That the Senate passed with an amendment H.R. 1625.
       That the Senate agreed to without amendment H. Con. Res. 
     106.
       With best wishes, I am,
           Sincerely,
     Karen L. Haas.
