    CONGRESSIONAL EARMARKS, LIMITED TAX BENEFITS, OR LIMITED TARIFF 
                                BENEFITS

  Under clause 9 of rule XXI, lists or statements on congressional 
earmarks, limited tax benefits, or limited tariff benefits were 
submitted as follows:

       The amendment to be offered by Representative Tsongas (MA), 
     or a designee, to H.R. 218, the King Cove Road Land Exchange 
     Act, does not contain any congressional earmarks, limited tax 
     benefits, or limited tariff benefits as defined in clause 9 
     of rule XXI.
       The amendment to be offered by me, Congressman Don Young 
     (AK), or a designee, to H.R. 218 does not contain any 
     congressional earmarks, limited tax benefits, or limited 
     tariff benefits as defined in clause 9 of rule XXI.
       The amendment to be offered by Representative Engel (NY), 
     or a designee, to H.R. 2883, the Promoting Cross-Border 
     Energy Infrastructure Act, does not contain any congressional 
     earmarks, limited tax benefits, or limited tariff benefits as 
     defined in clause 9 of rule XXI.
