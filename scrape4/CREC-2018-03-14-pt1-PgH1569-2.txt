PERMISSION FOR MEMBER TO BE CONSIDERED AS FIRST SPONSOR OF H. CON. RES. 
                                   79

  Mr. NADLER. Mr. Speaker, I ask unanimous consent that I may hereafter 
be considered to be the first sponsor of H. Con. Res. 79, a bill 
originally introduced by Representative Conyers of Michigan, for the 
purposes of adding cosponsors and requesting reprintings pursuant to 
clause 7 of rule XII.
  The SPEAKER pro tempore (Mr. Rogers of Kentucky). Is there objection 
to the request of the gentleman from New York?
  There was no objection.
