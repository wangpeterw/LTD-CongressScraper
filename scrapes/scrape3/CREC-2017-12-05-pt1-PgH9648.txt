             RESIGNATION FROM THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES

  The SPEAKER pro tempore laid before the House the following 
resignation from the House of Representatives:

                                     House of Representatives,

                                 Washington, DC, December 5, 2017.
     Hon. Paul Ryan,
     Speaker, House of Representatives,
     Washington, DC.
       Dear Mr. Speaker: I have made the decision to retire from 
     my position as the Member of the United States House of 
     Representatives from Michigan's Thirteenth Congressional 
     District effective today.
           Sincerely,
     John Conyers, Jr.
                                  ____



                                     House of Representatives,

                                 Washington, DC, December 5, 2017.
     Hon. Rick Snyder,
     Governor of Michigan,
     Lansing, MI.
       Dear Governor Snyder: I have made the decision to retire 
     from my position as the Member of the United States House of 
     Representatives from Michigan's Thirteenth Congressional 
     District effective today.
           Sincerely,
     John Conyers, Jr.
