                      PUBLIC BILLS AND RESOLUTIONS

  Under clause 2 of rule XII,

            Ms. JACKSON LEE introduced a bill (H.R. 3647) to amend 
             the Immigration and Nationality Act comprehensively 
             to reform immigration law, and for other purposes; to 
             the Committee on the Judiciary, and in addition to 
             the Committees on Homeland Security, and Oversight 
             and Government Reform, for a period to be 
             subsequently determined by the Speaker, in each case 
             for consideration of such provisions as fall within 
             the jurisdiction of the committee concerned.
