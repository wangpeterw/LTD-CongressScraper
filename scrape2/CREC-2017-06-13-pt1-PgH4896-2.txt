                            VERIFY FIRST ACT

  The SPEAKER pro tempore. The unfinished business is the vote on the 
motion to recommit on the bill (H.R. 2581) to amend the Internal 
Revenue Code of 1986 to require the provision of social security 
numbers as a condition of receiving the health insurance premium tax 
credit, offered by the gentlewoman from California (Ms. Sanchez), on 
which the yeas and nays were ordered.
  The Clerk will redesignate the motion.
  The Clerk redesignated the motion.
  The SPEAKER pro tempore. The question is on the motion to recommit.
  The vote was taken by electronic device, and there were--yeas 193, 
nays 231, not voting 6, as follows:
