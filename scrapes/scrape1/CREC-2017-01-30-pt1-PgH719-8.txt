   REPORT ON RESOLUTION PROVIDING FOR CONSIDERATION OF H.J. RES. 38, 
    DISAPPROVING A RULE SUBMITTED BY THE DEPARTMENT OF THE INTERIOR

  Mr. NEWHOUSE, from the Committee on Rules, submitted a privileged 
report (Rept. No. 115-6) on the resolution (H. Res. 70) providing for 
consideration of the joint resolution (H.J. Res. 38) disapproving the 
rule submitted by the Department of the Interior known as the Stream 
Protection Rule, which was referred to the House Calendar and ordered 
to be printed.
