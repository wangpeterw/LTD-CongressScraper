                  ORDERS FOR WEDNESDAY, MARCH 15, 2017

  Mr. INHOFE. Mr. President, I ask unanimous consent that when the 
Senate completes its business today, it adjourn until 9:30 a.m., 
Wednesday, March 15; further, that following the prayer and pledge, the 
morning hour be deemed expired, the Journal of proceedings be approved 
to date, the time for the two leaders be reserved for their use later 
in the day, and morning business be closed; finally, that following 
leader remarks, the Senate proceed to executive session as under the 
previous order.
  The PRESIDING OFFICER. Without objection, it is so ordered.
