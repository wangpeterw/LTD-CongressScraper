PROVIDING FOR CONSIDERATION OF H.R. 1039, PROBATION OFFICER PROTECTION 
                              ACT OF 2017

  The SPEAKER pro tempore. The unfinished business is the vote on 
ordering the previous question on the resolution (H. Res. 324) 
providing for consideration of the bill (H.R. 1039) to amend section 
3606 of title 18, United States Code, to grant probation officers 
authority to arrest hostile third parties who obstruct or impede a 
probation officer in the performance of official duties, on which the 
yeas and nays were ordered.
  The Clerk read the title of the resolution.
  The SPEAKER pro tempore. The question is on ordering the previous 
question.
