APPOINTMENT OF INDIVIDUAL TO BOARD OF TRUSTEES FOR THE JOHN C. STENNIS 
           CENTER FOR PUBLIC SERVICE TRAINING AND DEVELOPMENT

  The SPEAKER pro tempore. The Chair announces the Speaker's 
appointment, pursuant to section 114(b) of the John C. Stennis Center 
for Public Service Training and Development Act (2 U.S.C. 1103), and 
the order of the House of January 3, 2017, of the following individual 
on the part of the House to the Board of Trustees for the John C. 
Stennis Center for Public Service Training and Development for a term 
of 6 years:
  Mrs. Martha Roby, Montgomery, Alabama
