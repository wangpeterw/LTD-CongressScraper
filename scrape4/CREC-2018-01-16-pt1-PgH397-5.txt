  PERMISSION FOR MEMBER TO BE CONSIDERED AS FIRST SPONSOR OF H.R. 1661

  Mr. CURBELO of Florida. Mr. Speaker, I ask unanimous consent that I 
may hereafter be considered to be the first sponsor of H.R. 1661, a 
bill originally introduced by Representative Tiberi of Ohio, for the 
purposes of adding cosponsors and requesting reprintings pursuant to 
clause 7 of rule XII.
  The SPEAKER pro tempore. Is there objection to the request of the 
gentleman from Florida?
  There was no objection.
