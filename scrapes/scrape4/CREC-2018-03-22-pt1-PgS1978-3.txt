       PROVIDING FOR A CORRECTION IN THE ENROLLMENT OF H.R. 1625

  Mr. McCONNELL. Mr. President, I ask unanimous consent that the Senate 
proceed to the immediate consideration of H. Con. Res. 116, which was 
received from the House.
  The PRESIDING OFFICER. The clerk will report the concurrent 
resolution by title.
  The senior assistant legislative clerk read as follows:

       A concurrent resolution (H. Con. Res. 116) providing for a 
     correction in the enrollment of H.R. 1625.

  There being no objection, the Senate proceeded to consider the 
concurrent resolution.
  Mr. McCONNELL. I ask unanimous consent that the amendment at the desk 
be agreed to, the concurrent resolution, as amended, be agreed to, and 
the motion to reconsider be considered made and laid upon the table 
with no intervening action or debate.
  The PRESIDING OFFICER. Without objection, it is so ordered.
  The amendment (No. 2226) was agreed to, as follows:
       At the end add the following:
       ``On page 749, line 12, strike `and' through line 14 `are' 
     and insert `is' ''

  The concurrent resolution (H. Con. Res. 116), as amended, was agreed 
to.
  The PRESIDING OFFICER. The majority leader.
