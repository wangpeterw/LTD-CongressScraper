            By Mr. BRAT:
        H.J. Res. 119.
        Congress has the power to enact this legislation pursuant 
     to the following:
       Article V of the Constitution states that ``The Congress, 
     whenever two thirds of both Houses shall deem it necessary, 
     shall propose Amendments to this Constitution . . .'' This 
     joint resolution is submitted for Congress to consider 
     whether it is necessary to amend the Constitution to include 
     it.
