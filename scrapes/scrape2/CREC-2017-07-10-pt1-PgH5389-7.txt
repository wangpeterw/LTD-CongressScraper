                              ADJOURNMENT

  The SPEAKER pro tempore. Pursuant to section 2(b) of House Resolution

debate and 2 p.m. for legislative business.

order, the House adjourned until tomorrow, Tuesday, July 11, 2017, at 
noon for morning-hour debate.
