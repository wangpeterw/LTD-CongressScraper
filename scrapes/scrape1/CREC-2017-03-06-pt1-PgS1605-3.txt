                   ORDERS FOR TUESDAY, MARCH 7, 2017

  Mr. DAINES. Mr. President, I ask unanimous consent that when the 
Senate completes its business today, it adjourn until 10 a.m. on 
Tuesday, March 7; that following the prayer and pledge, the morning 
hour be deemed expired, the Journal of proceedings be approved to date, 
the time for the two leaders be reserved for their use later in the 
day, and morning business be closed; further, that following leader 
remarks, the Senate resume consideration of H.J. Res. 44; finally, that 
there be 8 hours of debate remaining on the resolution, equally divided 
in the usual form.
  The PRESIDING OFFICER. Without objection, it is so ordered.
