                         LETTER OF RESIGNATION

  Mr. SCHUMER. Mr. President, I ask unanimous consent that the letter 
of resignation from Lieutenant Governor Smith to Governor Dayton be 
printed in the Record.
  There being no objection, the letter was ordered to be printed in the 
Record, as follows:

                                               State of Minnesota,


                               Lieutenant Governor Tina Smith,

                                Saint Paul, MN, December 28, 2017.
     Hon. Mark Dayton,
     State Capitol,
     Saint Paul, MN.
       Dear Governor Dayton: Effective 11:59 p.m. January 2, 2018, 
     I hereby resign the Office of Lieutenant Governor of the 
     State of Minnesota. It has been an honor and privilege to 
     serve the people of Minnesota.
           Sincerely,
                                                       Tina Smith,
                                                     Lt. Governor.

  Mr. SCHUMER. I yield the floor.
