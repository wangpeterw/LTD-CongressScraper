REPORT ON RESOLUTION PROVIDING FOR CONSIDERATION OF H.R. 725, INNOCENT 
                          PARTY PROTECTION ACT

  Ms. CHENEY, from the Committee on Rules, submitted a privileged 
report (Rept. No. 115-27) on the resolution (H. Res. 175) providing for 
consideration of the bill (H.R. 725) to amend title 28, United States 
Code, to prevent fraudulent joinder, which was referred to the House 
Calendar and ordered to be printed.
