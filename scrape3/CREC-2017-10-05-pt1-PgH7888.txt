                               MEMORIALS

  Under clause 3 of rule XII,

       128. The SPEAKER presented a memorial of the Senate of the 
     State of Michigan, relative to Senate Resolution No. 82, 
     strongly denouncing and opposing the violent terrorism, 
     totalitarian impulses, xenophobic biases, and bigoted 
     ideologies that are promoted by radical hate groups and 
     declare these groups to be domestic terrorist organizations; 
     which was referred to the Committee on the Judiciary.
