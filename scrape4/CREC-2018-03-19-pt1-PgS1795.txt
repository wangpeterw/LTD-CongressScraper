                    AMENDMENTS SUBMITTED AND PROPOSED

       SA 2212. Mr. WYDEN submitted an amendment intended to be 
     proposed by him to the bill H.R. 1865, to amend the 
     Communications Act of 1934 to clarify that section 230 of 
     such Act does not prohibit the enforcement against providers 
     and users of interactive computer services of Federal and 
     State criminal and civil law relating to sexual exploitation 
     of children or sex trafficking, and for other purposes; which 
     was ordered to lie on the table.
       SA 2213. Mr. WYDEN submitted an amendment intended to be 
     proposed by him to the bill H.R. 1865, supra; which was 
     ordered to lie on the table.
       SA 2214. Mr. McCONNELL (for Mr. Grassley) proposed an 
     amendment to the bill H.R. 3731, to provide overtime pay for 
     employees of the United States Secret Service, and for other 
     purposes.
