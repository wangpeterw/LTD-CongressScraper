 AUTHORIZING THE CLERK TO MAKE CORRECTIONS IN ENGROSSMENT OF H.J. RES. 
 76, GRANTING CONSENT AND APPROVAL OF CONGRESS TO ESTABLISH WASHINGTON 
                      METRORAIL SAFETY COMMISSION

  The SPEAKER pro tempore. Without objection, the Clerk is directed to 
make the change in the engrossment of H.J. Res. 76 that has been placed 
at the desk.
  The Clerk read as follows:

       In the final whereas clause, strike the period at the end 
     and insert the following: ``: Now, therefore, be it''.

  There was no objection.
