                           MEASURES REFERRED

  The following bills were read the first and the second times by 
unanimous consent, and referred as indicated:

       H.R. 1761. An act to amend title 18, United States Code, to 
     criminalize the knowing consent of the visual depiction, or 
     live transmission, of a minor engaged in sexually explicit 
     conduct, and for other purposes; to the Committee on the 
     Judiciary.
       H.R. 1973. An act to prevent the sexual abuse of minors and 
     amateur athletes by requiring the prompt reporting of sexual 
     abuse to law enforcement authorities, and for other purposes; 
     to the Committee on the Judiciary.
