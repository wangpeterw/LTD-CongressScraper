             END BANKING FOR HUMAN TRAFFICKERS ACT OF 2018

  The SPEAKER pro tempore. The unfinished business is the vote on the 
motion to suspend the rules and pass the bill (H.R. 2219) to increase 
the role of the financial industry in combating human trafficking, as 
amended, on which the yeas and nays were ordered.
  The Clerk read the title of the bill.
  The SPEAKER pro tempore. The question is on the motion offered by the 
gentleman from California (Mr. Royce) that the House suspend the rules 
and pass the bill, as amended.
  The vote was taken by electronic device, and there were--yeas 408, 
nays 2, not voting 18, as follows:
