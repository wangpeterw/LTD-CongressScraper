 ADJOURNMENT FROM FRIDAY, FEBRUARY 2, 2018, TO MONDAY, FEBRUARY 5, 2018

  Mr. THOMAS J. ROONEY of Florida. Mr. Speaker, I ask unanimous consent 
that when the House adjourns on Friday, February 2, 2018, it adjourn to 
meet on Monday, February 5, 2018, when it shall convene at noon for 
morning-hour debate and 2 p.m. for legislative business.

gentleman from Florida?
