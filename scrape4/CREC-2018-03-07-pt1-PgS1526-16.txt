                 RUTLEDGE PEARSON POST OFFICE BUILDING

  The bill (H.R. 3638) to designate the facility of the United States 
Postal Service located at 1100 Kings Road in Jacksonville, Florida, as 
the ``Rutledge Pearson Post Office Building,'' was ordered to a third 
reading and was read the third time.
