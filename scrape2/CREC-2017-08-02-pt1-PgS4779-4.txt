                              WITHDRAWALS

  Executive Message transmitted by the President to the Senate on 
August 2, 2017 withdrawing from further Senate consideration the 
following nominations:

       GEORGE NESTERCZUK, OF VIRGINIA, TO BE DIRECTOR OF THE 
     OFFICE OF PERSONNEL MANAGEMENT FOR A TERM OF FOUR YEARS, VICE 
     KATHERINE ARCHULETA, RESIGNED, WHICH WAS SENT TO THE SENATE 
     ON MAY 25, 2017.
       JAMIE MCCOURT, OF CALIFORNIA, TO BE AMBASSADOR 
     EXTRAORDINARY AND PLENIPOTENTIARY OF THE UNITED STATES OF 
     AMERICA TO THE KINGDOM OF BELGIUM, WHICH WAS SENT TO THE 
     SENATE ON JUNE 26, 2017.
