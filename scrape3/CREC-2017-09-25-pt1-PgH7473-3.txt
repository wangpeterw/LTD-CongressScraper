    REPORT ON RESOLUTION PROVIDING FOR CONSIDERATION OF H.R. 2824, 
  INCREASING OPPORTUNITY AND SUCCESS FOR CHILDREN AND PARENTS THROUGH 
 EVIDENCE-BASED HOME VISITING ACT; PROVIDING FOR CONSIDERATION OF H.R. 
   2792, CONTROL UNLAWFUL FUGITIVE FELONS ACT OF 2017; AND FOR OTHER 
                                PURPOSES

  Mr. BURGESS, from the Committee on Rules, submitted a privileged 
report (Rept. No. 115-331) on the resolution (H. Res. 533) providing 
for consideration of the bill (H.R. 2824) to amend title V of the 
Social Security Act to extend the Maternal, Infant, and Early Childhood 
Home Visiting Program; providing for consideration of the bill (H.R. 
2792) to amend the Social Security Act to make certain revisions to 
provisions limiting payment of benefits to fugitive felons under titles 
II, VIII, and XVI of the Social Security Act; and for other purposes, 
which was referred to the House Calendar and ordered to be printed.
