 REPORT ON RESOLUTION PROVIDING FOR CONSIDERATION OF H.R. 2201, MICRO 
                        OFFERING SAFE HARBOR ACT

  Mr. SESSIONS, from the Committee on Rules, submitted a privileged 
report (Rept. No. 115-401) on the resolution (H. Res. 609) providing 
for consideration of the bill (H.R. 2201) to amend the Securities Act 
of 1933 to exempt certain micro-offerings from the registration 
requirements of such Act, and for other purposes, which was referred to 
the House Calendar and ordered to be printed.
