                    ENROLLED JOINT RESOLUTION SIGNED

  Karen L. Haas, Clerk of the House, reported and found truly enrolled 
a joint resolution of the House of the following title, which was 
thereupon signed by the Speaker on Monday, August 7, 2017:

       H.J. Res 76. Joint resolution granting the consent and 
     approval of Congress for the Commonwealth of Virginia, the 
     State of Maryland, and the District of Columbia to enter into 
     a compact relating to the establishment of the Washington 
     Metrorail Safety Commission.
