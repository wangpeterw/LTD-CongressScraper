              ENDY NDDIOBONG EKPANYA POST OFFICE BUILDING

  The bill (H.R. 294) to designate the facility of the United States 
Postal Service located at 2700 Cullen Boulevard in Pearland, Texas, as 
the ``Endy Nddiobong Ekpanya Post Office Building,'' was ordered to a 
third reading and was read the third time.
