                       GEORGE SAKATO POST OFFICE

  The bill (S. 931) to designate the facility of the United States 
Postal Service located at 4910 Brighton Boulevard in Denver, Colorado, 
as the ``George Sakato Post Office,'' was ordered to be engrossed for a 
third reading and was read the third time.
