                    DISCHARGE AND REFERRAL--H.R. 590

  Mr. BOOZMAN. Madam President, I ask unanimous consent that H.R. 590, 
the Advanced Nuclear Technology Development Act of 2017, be discharged 
from the Committee on Commerce, Science, and Transportation and 
referred to the Committee on Environment and Public Works.
  The PRESIDING OFFICER. Without objection, it is so ordered.
