           SUPERIOR NATIONAL FOREST LAND EXCHANGE ACT OF 2017

  The SPEAKER pro tempore. The unfinished business is the vote on the 
motion to suspend the rules and pass the bill (H.R. 3115) to provide 
for a land exchange involving Federal land in the Superior National 
Forest in Minnesota acquired by the Secretary of Agriculture through 
the Weeks Law, and for other purposes, as amended, on which the yeas 
and nays were ordered.
  The Clerk read the title of the bill.
  The SPEAKER pro tempore. The question is on the motion offered by the 
gentleman from Colorado (Mr. Tipton) that the House suspend the rules 
and pass the bill, as amended.
  This is a 5-minute vote.
  The vote was taken by electronic device, and there were--yeas 309, 
nays 99, not voting 25, as follows:
