      MARTIN LUTHER KING, JR. NATIONAL HISTORICAL PARK ACT OF 2017

  The SPEAKER pro tempore (Mr. Arrington). The unfinished business is 
the question on suspending the rules and passing the bill (H.R. 267) to 
redesignate the Martin Luther King, Junior, National Historic Site in 
the State of Georgia, and for other purposes, as amended.
  The Clerk read the title of the bill.
  The SPEAKER pro tempore. The question is on the motion offered by the 
gentleman from Colorado (Mr. Tipton) that the House suspend the rules 
and pass the bill, as amended.
  The question was taken; and (two-thirds being in the affirmative) the 
rules were suspended and the bill, as amended, was passed.
  A motion to reconsider was laid on the table.
