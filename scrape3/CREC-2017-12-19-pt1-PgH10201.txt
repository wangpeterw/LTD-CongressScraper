AUTHORIZING THE CLERK TO MAKE CORRECTIONS IN ENGROSSMENT OF H.R. 4375, 
     STEM RESEARCH AND EDUCATION EFFECTIVENESS AND TRANSPARENCY ACT

  Mrs. COMSTOCK. Mr. Speaker, I ask unanimous consent that the Clerk be 
directed to make the change in the engrossment of H.R. 4375 that I have 
placed at the table.
  The SPEAKER pro tempore. The Clerk will report the modification.
  The Clerk read as follows:

       Amend the title so as to read: ``A bill to provide for a 
     report on broadening participation in certain National 
     Science Foundation research and education programs, to 
     collect data on Federal research grants to science agencies, 
     and for other purposes.''.

  The SPEAKER pro tempore. Is there objection to the request of the 
gentlewoman from Virginia?
  There was no objection.
