        ENDANGERED FISH RECOVERY PROGRAMS EXTENSION ACT OF 2017

  The SPEAKER pro tempore. The unfinished business is the vote on the 
motion to suspend the rules and pass the bill (H.R. 4465) to maintain 
annual base funding for the Upper Colorado and San Juan fish recovery 
programs through fiscal year 2023, to require a report on the 
implementation of those programs, and for other purposes, on which the 
yeas and nays were ordered.
  The Clerk read the title of the bill.
  The SPEAKER pro tempore. The question is on the motion offered by the 
gentleman from Utah (Mr. Curtis) that the House suspend the rules and 
pass the bill.
  This is a 5-minute vote.
  The vote was taken by electronic device, and there were--yeas 392, 
nays 6, not voting 32, as follows:
