 AMENDING THE DELAWARE WATER GAP NATIONAL RECREATION AREA IMPROVEMENT 
                                  ACT

  The bill (H.R. 560) to amend the Delaware Water Gap National 
Recreation Area Improvement Act to provide access to certain vehicles 
serving residents of municipalities adjacent to the Delaware Water Gap 
National Recreation Area, and for other purposes, was ordered to a 
third reading and was read the third time.
