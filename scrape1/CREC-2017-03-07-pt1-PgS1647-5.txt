                  ORDERS FOR WEDNESDAY, MARCH 8, 2017

  Mr. LEE. Mr. President, I ask unanimous consent that when the Senate 
completes its business today, it adjourn until 9:30 a.m. on Wednesday, 
March 8; that following the prayer and pledge, the morning hour be 
deemed expired, the Journal of proceedings be approved to date, the 
time for the two leaders be reserved for their use later in the day, 
and morning business be closed; finally, that following leader remarks, 
the Senate resume consideration of H.J. Res. 58.
  The PRESIDING OFFICER. Without objection, it is so ordered.
