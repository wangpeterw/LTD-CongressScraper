               GUNNERY SERGEANT JOHN BASILONE POST OFFICE

  The bill (H.R. 2815) to designate the facility of the United States 
Postal Service located at 30 East Somerset Street in Raritan, New 
Jersey, as the ``Gunnery Sergeant John Basilone Post Office,'' was 
ordered to a third reading and was read the third time.
