                         MESSAGE FROM THEHOUSE

  At 12:53 p.m., a message from the House of Representatives, delivered 
by Mrs. Cole, one of its reading clerks,announced that the House has 
passed the following bills, in which it requests the concurrence of the 
Senate:

       H.R. 5. An act to reform the process by which Federal 
     agencies analyze and formulate new regulations and 
     guidancedocuments, to clarify the nature of judicial review 
     of agency interpretations, to ensure complete analysis of 
     potential impactson small entities of rules, and for other 
     purposes.
       H.R. 39. An act to amend title 5, United StatesCode, to 
     codify the Presidential Innovation Fellows Program, and for 
     other purposes.
