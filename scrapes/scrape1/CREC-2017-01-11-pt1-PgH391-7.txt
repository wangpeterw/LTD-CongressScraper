            By Mr. DENHAM:
        H.R. 434.
        Congress has the power to enact this legislation pursuant 
     to the following:
       Article I, Section 8of the United States Constitution, 
     specifically Clause 1 (relating to providing for he common 
     defense and general welfare of theUnited States), Clause 3 
     (relating to regulating commerce with foreign nations, and 
     among the several states, and with theIndian tribes) and 
     Clause 18 (relating to the power to make all laws necessary 
     and proper for carrying out the powers vested inCongress).
