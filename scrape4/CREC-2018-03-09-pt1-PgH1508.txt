                            PETITIONS, ETC.

  Under clause 3 of rule XII, petitions and papers were laid on the 
clerk's desk and referred as follows:

       86. The SPEAKER presented a petition of the Legislature of 
     Erie County, New York, relative to a resolution, urging both 
     Chambers of Congress to work together to fund the Children's 
     Health Insurance Program (CHIP), and in doing so upholding 
     their civic commitment to protect and safeguard our nation's 
     children from sickness and illness; to the Committee on 
     Energy and Commerce.
       87. Also, a petition of the Legislature of Erie County, New 
     York, relative to a resolution, declaring strong support for 
     Senator Charles Schumer's efforts to include additional 
     federal funding in the forthcoming federal budget bill to 
     help address the opioid epidemic in the United States; to the 
     Committee on Energy and Commerce.
       88. Also, a petition of the City of Council of Gautier, 
     Mississippi, relative to Resolution Number 8-2018, supporting 
     continued and increased exploration and production of the 
     Gulf of Mexico, and urging the Bureau of Ocean Energy 
     Management to finalize a 2019-2024 National OCS Program that 
     maintains and expands access to Gulf of Mexico energy 
     resources; to the Committee on Natural Resources.
