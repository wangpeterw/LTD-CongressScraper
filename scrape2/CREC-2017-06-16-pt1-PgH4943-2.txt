                               MEMORIALS

  Under clause 3 of rule XII,

       68. The SPEAKER presented a memorial of the Legislature of 
     the State of Louisiana, relative to Senate Concurrent 
     Resolution No. 22, memorializing the Congress to take such 
     actions as are necessary to review the Government Pension 
     Offset and the Windfall Elimination Provision Social Security 
     benefit reductions and to consider eliminating or reducing 
     them; which was referred to the Committee on Ways and Means.
