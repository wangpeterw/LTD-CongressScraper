                                 RECESS

  The SPEAKER pro tempore. Pursuant to clause 12(a) of rule I, the 
Chair declares the House in recess for a period of less than 15 
minutes.
  Accordingly (at 3 o'clock and 24 minutes p.m.), the House stood in 
recess.
