                         PARLIAMENTARY INQUIRY

  Mr. RASKIN. Mr. Speaker, I have a parliamentary inquiry.
  The SPEAKER pro tempore. The gentleman will state his parliamentary 
inquiry.
  Mr. RASKIN. Mr. Speaker, does the chairman of the House Intelligence 
Committee have the authority to transmit a classified document under 
House rule X, clause 11(g) that was never approved for release by a 
vote of the committee?
  The SPEAKER pro tempore. The Chair will not issue an advisory 
opinion.
  Mr. RASKIN. Even if it relates to current events?
  The SPEAKER pro tempore. The Chair will not issue an advisory 
opinion.
