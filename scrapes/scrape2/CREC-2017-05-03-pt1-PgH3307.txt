           MOMENT OF SILENCE IN MEMORY OF MATTHEW McCLANAHAN

  The SPEAKER. The Chair asks that the House now observe a moment of 
silence in memory of Matthew McClanahan, a pipefitter for the Architect 
of the Capitol who died in a tragic accident on the Capitol Grounds on 
April 18.
