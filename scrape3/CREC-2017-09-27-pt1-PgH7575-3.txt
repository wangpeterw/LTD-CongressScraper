                    BILL PRESENTED TO THE PRESIDENT

  Karen L. Haas, Clerk of the House, reported that on September 26, 
2017, she presented to the President of the United States, for his 
approval, the following bill:

       H.R. 3110. To amend the Financial Stability Act of 2010 to 
     modify the term of the independent member of the Financial 
     Stability Oversight Council.
