REPORT ON RESOLUTION PROVIDING FOR FURTHER CONSIDERATION OF H.R. 1628, 
                    AMERICAN HEALTH CARE ACT OF 2017

  Mr. SESSIONS, from the Committee on Rules, submitted a privileged 
report (Rept. No. 115-88) on the resolution (H. Res. 254) providing for 
further consideration of the bill (H.R. 1628) to provide for 
reconciliation pursuant to title II of the concurrent resolution on the 
budget for fiscal year 2017, which was referred to the House Calendar 
and ordered to be printed.
