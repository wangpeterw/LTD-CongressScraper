                    BILLS PRESENTED TO THE PRESIDENT

  Karen L. Haas, Clerk of the House, reported that on October 24, 2017, 
she presented to the President of the United States, for his approval, 
the following bills:

       H.R. 2989. To establish the Frederick Douglass Bicentennial 
     Commission.
       H.R. 1616. To amend the Homeland Security Act of 2002 to 
     authorize the National Computer Forensics Institute, and for 
     other purposes.
