                     EXECUTIVE REPORTS OF COMMITTEE

  The following executive reports of nominations were submitted:

       By Ms. MURKOWSKI for the Committee on Energy and Natural 
     Resources.
       * David Bernhardt, of Virginia, to be Deputy Secretary of 
     the Interior.
       * Neil Chatterjee, of Kentucky, to be a Member of the 
     Federal Energy Regulatory Commission for the term expiring 
     June 30, 2021.
       * Robert F. Powelson, of Pennsylvania, to be a Member of 
     the Federal Energy Regulatory Commission for the term 
     expiring June 30, 2020.
       * Dan R. Brouillette, of Texas, to be Deputy Secretary of 
     Energy.

  * Nomination was reported with recommendation that it be confirmed 
subject to the nominee's commitment to respond to requests to appear 
and testify before any duly constituted committee of the Senate.
