                         TRIBUTE TO ZANE HEHNKE

 Mrs. ERNST. Mr. President, today I wish to recognize the 
distinguished accomplishment of Mr. Zane Hehnke of Winterset, IA, a 
2017 finalist for the NFIB Young Entrepreneur of the Year Award.
  Mr. Hehnke, of Winterset, IA, is the founder and owner of Inspired 
Finds, an interior design company which specializes in designing and 
creating accents using vintage and antique items.
  I ask my colleagues to join me as I proudly recognize Mr. Hehnke on 
his outstanding achievements. We wish him nothing but the best in his 
future entrepreneurial and academic pursuits.
