                               MEMORIALS

  Under clause 3 of rule XII, memorials were presented and referred as 
follows:

       164. The SPEAKER presented a memorial of the General 
     Assembly of the State of Colorado, relative to House Joint 
     Resolution 18-1004, recognizing the bravery and sacrifice of 
     the crew of the U.S.S. Pueblo on the Fiftieth Anniversary of 
     the capture of the U.S.S. Pueblo by North Korea; jointly to 
     the Committees on Armed Services and Foreign Affairs.
       165. Also, a memorial of the General Assembly of the State 
     of New Jersey, relative to Assembly Resolution No. 117, 
     urging the U.S. Congress and the Centers for Medicare and 
     Medicaid Services (CMS) to take appropriate action to ensure 
     that new teaching hospitals are timely provided with annual 
     federal funding for graduate medical education (GME); jointly 
     to the Committees on Energy and Commerce and Ways and Means.
