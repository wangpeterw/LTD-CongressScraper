                COMMUNICATION FROM THE DEMOCRATIC LEADER

  The SPEAKER pro tempore (Mr. Comer) laid before the House the 
following communication from the Honorable Nancy Pelosi, Democratic 
Leader:

                                                 February 7, 2017.
     Hon. Paul D. Ryan,
     Speaker of the House, U.S. Capitol,
     Washington, DC.
       Dear Speaker Ryan: Pursuant to section 4(b) of House 
     Resolution 5, 115th Congress, I am pleased to reappoint The 
     Honorable James P. McGovern of Massachusetts as Co-Chair of 
     the Tom Lantos Human Rights Commission.
       Thank you for your attention to this appointment.
           Best regards,
                                                     Nancy Pelosi,
     Democratic Leader.
