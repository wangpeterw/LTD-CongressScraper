REPORT ON RESOLUTION PROVIDING FOR CONSIDERATION OF H.R. 7, NO TAXPAYER 
FUNDING FOR ABORTION AND ABORTION INSURANCE FULL DISCLOSURE ACT OF 2017

report (Rept. No. 115-5) on the resolution (H. Res. 55) providing for 
consideration of the bill (H.R. 7) to prohibit taxpayer funded 
abortions, which was referred to the House Calendar and ordered to be
