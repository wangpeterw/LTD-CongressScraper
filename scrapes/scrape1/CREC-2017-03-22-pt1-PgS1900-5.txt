  DISAPPROVING A RULE SUBMITTED BY THE DEPARTMENT OF LABOR--MOTION TO 
                                PROCEED

  Mr. McCONNELL. Mr. President, I move to proceed to H.J. Res. 83.
  The ACTING PRESIDENT pro tempore. The clerk will report the motion.
  The senior assistant legislative clerk read as follows:

       Motion to proceed to H.J. Res. 83, a joint resolution 
     disapproving the rule submitted by the Department of Labor 
     relating to ``Clarification of Employer's Continuing 
     Obligation to Make and Maintain an Accurate Record of Each 
     Recordable Injury and Illness.''

  The ACTING PRESIDENT pro tempore. The question is on agreeing to the 
motion to proceed.
  The motion was agreed to.
