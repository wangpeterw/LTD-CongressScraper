                               MEMORIALS

  Under clause 3 of rule XII, memorials were presented and referred as 
follows:

       124. The SPEAKER presented a memorial of the General 
     Assembly of the State of New Jersey, relative to Assembly 
     Resolution No. 198, urging the President and the Congress of 
     the United States to continue the partnership which exists 
     between the State and Federal Government to manage the 
     Medicaid program; to the Committee on Energy and Commerce.
       125. Also, a memorial of the General Assembly of the State 
     of New Jersey, relative to Assembly Resolution No. 212, 
     urging Congress and the President to enact legislation that 
     creates a federal tax credit for hiring new apprentices; to 
     the Committee on Ways and Means.
