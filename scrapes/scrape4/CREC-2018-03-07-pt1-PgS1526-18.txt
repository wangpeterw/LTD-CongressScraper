                     ZACK T. ADDINGTON POST OFFICE

  The bill (H.R. 3821) to designate the facility of the United States 
Postal Service located at 430 Main Street in Clermont, Georgia, as the 
``Zack T. Addington Post Office,'' was ordered to a third reading and 
was read the third time.
