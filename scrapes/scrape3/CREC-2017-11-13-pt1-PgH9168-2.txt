                         SENATE BILLS REFERRED

  Bills of the Senate of the following titles were taken from the 
Speaker's table and, under the rule, referred as follows:

       S. 1153. An act to prohibit or suspend certain health care 
     providers from providing non-Department of Veterans Affairs 
     health care services to veterans, and for other purposes; to 
     the Committee on Veterans' Affairs.
       S. 1266. An act to authorize the Secretary of Veterans 
     Affairs to enter into contracts with nonprofit organizations 
     to investigate medical centers of the Department of Veterans 
     Affairs; to the Committee on Veterans' Affairs.
