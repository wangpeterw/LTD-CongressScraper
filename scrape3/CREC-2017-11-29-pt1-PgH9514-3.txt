REPORT ON RESOLUTION PROVIDING FOR CONSIDERATION OF H.R. 4182, ENSURING 
A QUALIFIED CIVIL SERVICE ACT OF 2017, AND PROVIDING FOR CONSIDERATION 
  OF H.R. 1699, PRESERVING ACCESS TO MANUFACTURED HOUSING ACT OF 2017

  Mr. WOODALL, from the Committee on Rules, submitted a privileged 
report (Rept. No. 115-430) on the resolution (H. Res. 635) providing 
for consideration of the bill (H.R. 4182) to amend title 5, United 
States Code, to modify probationary periods with respect to positions 
within the competitive service and the Senior Executive Service, and 
for other purposes, and providing for consideration of the bill (H.R. 
1699) to amend the Truth in Lending Act to modify the definitions of a 
mortgage originator and a high-cost mortgage, to amend the Secure and 
Fair Enforcement for Mortgage Licensing Act of 2008 to modify the 
definition of a loan originator, and for other purposes, which was 
referred to the House Calendar and ordered to be printed.
