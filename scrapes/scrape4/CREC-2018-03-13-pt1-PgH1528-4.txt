      REQUESTING RETURN OF H.R. 1207, TILDEN VETERANS POST OFFICE

  The SPEAKER pro tempore laid before the House the following 
privileged message from the Senate:

       In the Senate of the United States, March 12, 2018.
       Ordered, That the Secretary be directed to request the 
     House of Representatives to return to the Senate the bill 
     (H.R. 1207) entitled ``An Act to designate the facility of 
     the United States Postal Service located at 306 River Street 
     in Tilden, Texas, as the `Tilden Veterans Post Office'.''.

           Attest:
                                                   Julie E. Adams,
                                                        Secretary.

  The SPEAKER pro tempore. Without objection, the request is granted.
  There was no objection.
