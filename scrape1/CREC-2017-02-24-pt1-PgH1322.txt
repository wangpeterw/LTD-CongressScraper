                            PETITIONS, ETC.

  Under clause 3 of rule XII, petitions and papers were laid on the 
clerk's desk and referred as follows:

       17. The SPEAKER presented a petition of The Trust for 
     Public Land, San Francisco, CA, relative to seven petitions 
     from private citizens in opposition to Congress's attempts to 
     sell off public lands and turn them over to states to manage; 
     to the Committee on Natural Resources.
       18. Also, a petition of New Orleans City Council, relative 
     to Resolution No. R-17-79, condemning the Executive Order 
     entitled ``Protection of the Nation From Foreign Terrorist 
     Entry Into The United States'', issued January 27, 2017; 
     jointly to the Committees on the Judiciary, Foreign Affairs, 
     Homeland Security, and Intelligence (Permanent Select).
