    REPORT ON RESOLUTION PROVIDING FOR CONSIDERATION OF H.R. 1343, 
               ENCOURAGING EMPLOYEE OWNERSHIP ACT OF 2017

  Mr. BUCK, from the Committee on Rules, submitted a privileged report 
(Rept. No. 115-75) on the resolution (H. Res. 240) providing for 
consideration of the bill (H.R. 1343) to direct the Securities and 
Exchange Commission to revise its rules so as to increase the threshold 
amount for requiring issuers to provide certain disclosures relating to 
compensatory benefit plans, which was referred to the House Calendar 
and ordered to be printed.
