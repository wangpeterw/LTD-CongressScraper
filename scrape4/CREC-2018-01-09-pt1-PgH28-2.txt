                        MESSAGE FROM THE SENATE

  A message from the Senate by Ms. Lasky, one of its clerks, announced 
that the Senate has passed without amendment a bill of the House of the 
following title:

       H.R. 3759. An act to provide for the establishment and 
     maintenance of a Family Caregiving Strategy, and for other 
     purposes.

  The message also announced that the Senate has passed a bill of the 
following title in which the concurrence of the House is requested:

       S. 1425. An act to reauthorize the Integrated Coastal and 
     Ocean Observation System Act of 2009, and for other purposes.
