  HOUR OF MEETING ON TOMORROW, AND ADJOURNMENT FROM FRIDAY, MARCH 9, 
                    2018, TO TUESDAY, MARCH 13, 2018

  Mr. McCARTHY. Mr. Speaker, I ask unanimous consent that when the 
House adjourns today, it adjourn to meet at 10:30 a.m. tomorrow, and 
further, when the House adjourns on that day, it adjourn to meet on 
Tuesday, March 13, 2018, when it shall convene at noon for morning-hour 
debate and 2 p.m. for legislative business.
  The SPEAKER pro tempore (Mr. Mitchell). Is there objection to the 
request of the gentleman from California?
  There was no objection.
