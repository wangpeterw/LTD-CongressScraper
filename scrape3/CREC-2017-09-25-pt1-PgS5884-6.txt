                         MESSAGE FROM THE HOUSE

  At 4:03 p.m., a message from the House of Representatives, delivered

passed the following bill, in which it requests the concurrence of the 
Senate:

       H.R. 3354. An act making appropriations for the Department 
     of the Interior, environment, and related agencies for the 
     fiscal year ending September 30, 2018, and for other 
     purposes.
