    CONGRESSIONAL EARMARKS, LIMITED TAX BENEFITS, OR LIMITED TARIFF 
                                BENEFITS

  Under clause 9 of rule XXI, lists or statements on congressional 
earmarks, limited tax benefits, or limited tariff benefits were 
submitted as follows:

                          Offered By Mr. Brady

       The provisions that warranted a referral to the Committee 
     on Ways and Means in H.R. 7 do not contain any congressional 
     earmarks, limited tax benefits, or limited tariff benefits as 
     defined in clause 9 of rule XXI.

                        Offered By Mr. Goodlatte

       The provisions that warranted a referral to the Committee 
     on Judiciary in H.R. 7 do not contain any congressional 
     earmarks, limited tax benefits, or limited tariff benefits as 
     defined in clause 9 of rule XXI.

                         Offered By Mr. Walden

       The provisions that warranted a referral to the Committee 
     on Energy and Commerce in H.R. 7 do not contain any 
     congressional earmarks, limited tax benefits, or limited 
     tariff benefits as defined in clause 9 of rule XXI.
