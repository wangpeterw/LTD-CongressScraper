     Competition Bureau, Federal Communications Commission,

       869. A letter from the Assistant Legal Adviser, Office of

     Affairs.
       870. A letter from the Director, Defense Security 
     Cooperation Agency, Department of Defense, transmitting the 
     Air Force's proposed Letter of Offer and Acceptance to the 
     Government of the United Kingdom, Transmittal No. 17-02,

     amended; to the Committee on Foreign Affairs.
       871. A letter from the Secretary, Department of the 
     Treasury, transmitting a notification of a determination 
     that, by reason of the statutory debt limit, the Secretary is 
     unable to comply with the investment requirements of the 
     Civil Service Retirement and Disability Fund, pursuant to 5 
     U.S.C. 8348(l)(2); Public Law 89-554, Sec. 8348(l)(2) (as 
     added by Public Law 99-509, Sec. 6002(c)); (100 Stat. 1933) 
     and 5 U.S.C. 8438(h)(2); Public Law 99-335, Sec. 101(a) (as 
     amended by Public Law 101-335, Sec. 3(a)(7)); (104 Stat.
